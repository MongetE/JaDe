1. Trusty, dusky, vivid, true,
2. With eyes of gold and bramble-dew,
3. Steel-true and blade-straight,
4. The great artificer
5. Made my mate.

6. Honour, anger, valour, fire;
7. A love that life could never tire,
8. Death quench or evil stir,
9. The mighty master
10. Gave to her.

11. Teacher, tender, comrade, wife,
12. A fellow-farer true through life,
13. Heart-whole and soul-free
14. The august father
15. Gave to me.
