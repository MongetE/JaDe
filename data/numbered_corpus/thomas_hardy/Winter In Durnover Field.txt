1. Scene.--A wide stretch of fallow ground recently sown with wheat, and
2. frozen to iron hardness. Three large birds walking about thereon,
3. and wistfully eyeing the surface. Wind keen from north-east: sky a
4. dull grey.

5. (Triolet)

6. Rook.--Throughout the field I find no grain;
7. The cruel frost encrusts the cornland!Starling.--Aye: patient pecking now is vain
8. Throughout the field, I find . . .
9. Rook.--No grain!
10. Pigeon.--Nor will be, comrade, till it rain,
11. Or genial thawings loose the lorn land
12. Throughout the field.
13. Rook.--I find no grain:
14. The cruel frost encrusts the cornland!
