1. I
2. Only a man harrowing clods
3. In a slow silent walk
4. With an old horse that stumbles and nods
5. Half asleep as they stalk.

6. II
7. Only thin smoke without flame
8. From the heaps of couch-grass;
9. Yet this will go onwards the same
10. Though Dynasties pass.

11. III
12. Yonder a maid and her wight
13. Go whispering by:
14. War's annals will cloud into night
15. Ere their story die.
