1. I

2. Never a careworn wife but shows,
3. If a joy suffuse her,
4. Something beautiful to those
5. Patient to peruse her,
6. Some one charm the world unknows
7. Precious to a muser,
8. Haply what, ere years were foes,
9. Moved her mate to choose her.

10. II

11. But, be it a hint of rose
12. That an instant hues her,
13. Or some early light or pose
14. Wherewith thought renews her -
15. Seen by him at full, ere woes
16. Practised to abuse her -
17. Sparely comes it, swiftly goes,
18. Time again subdues her.
