1. Minor Key

2. I

3. Let me enjoy the earth no less
4. Because the all-enacting Might
5. That fashioned forth its loveliness
6. Had other aims than my delight.

7. II

8. About my path there flits a Fair,
9. Who throws me not a word or sign;
10. I'll charm me with her ignoring air,
11. And laud the lips not meant for mine.

12. III

13. From manuscripts of moving song
14. Inspired by scenes and dreams unknown
15. I'll pour out raptures that belong
16. To others, as they were my own.

17. IV

18. And some day hence, towards Paradise
19. And all its blest -- if such should be --
20. I will lift glad, afar-off eyes
21. Though it contain no place for me.
