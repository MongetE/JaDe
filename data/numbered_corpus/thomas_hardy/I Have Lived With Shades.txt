1. I

2. I have lived with Shades so long,
3. So long have talked to them,
4. I sped to street and throng,
5. That sometimes they
6. In their dim style
7. Will pause awhile
8. To hear my say;

9. II

10. And take me by the hand,
11. And lead me through their rooms
12. In the To-Be, where Dooms
13. Half-wove and shapeless stand:
14. And show from there
15. The dwindled dust
16. And rot and rust
17. Of things that were.

18. III

19. "Now turn," they said to me
20. One day: "Look whence we came,
21. And signify his name
22. Who gazes thence at thee" --
23. -- "Nor name nor race
24. Know I, or can,"
25. I said, "Of man
26. So commonplace."

27. IV

28. "He moves me not at all:
29. I note no ray or jot
30. Of rareness in his lot,
31. Or star exceptional.
32. Into the dim
33. Dead throngs around
34. He'll sink, nor sound
35. Be left of him."

36. V

37. "Yet," said they, "his frail speech,
38. Hath accents pitched like thine --
39. Thy mould and his define
40. A likeness each to each --
41. But go! Deep pain
42. Alas, would be
43. His name to thee,
44. And told in vain!"
