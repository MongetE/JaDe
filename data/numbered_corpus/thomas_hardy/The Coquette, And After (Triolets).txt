1. I

2. For long the cruel wish I knew
3. That your free heart should ache for me
4. While mine should bear no ache for you;
5. For, long--the cruel wish!--I knew
6. How men can feel, and craved to view
7. My triumph--fated not to be
8. For long! . . . The cruel wish I knew
9. That your free heart should ache for me!

10. II

11. At last one pays the penalty -
12. The woman--women always do.
13. My farce, I found, was tragedy
14. At last!--One pays the penalty
15. With interest when one, fancy-free,
16. Learns love, learns shame . . . Of sinners two
17. At last ONE pays the penalty -
18. The woman--women always do!
