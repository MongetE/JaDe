1. WHEN Lawyers strive to heal a breach,
2. And Parsons practise what they preach;
3. Then Little Boney he'll pounce down,
4. And march his men on London town!
5. Rollicum-rorum, tol-lol-lorum,
6. Rollicum-rorum, tol-lol-lay!
7. When Justices hold equal scales,
8. And Rogues are only found in jails;
9. Then Little Boney he'll pounce down,
10. And march his men on London town!
11. Rollicum-rorum, etc.

12. When Rich Men find their wealth a curse,
13. And fill therewith the Poor Man's purse;
14. Then Little Boney he'll pounce down,
15. And march his men on London town!
16. Rollicum-rorum, etc.

17. When Husbands with their Wives agree,
18. And Maids won't wed from modesty;
19. Then Little Boney he'll pounce down,
20. And march his men on London town!
21. Rollicum-rorum, tol-lol-lorum,
22. Rollicum-rorum, tol-lol-lay!
