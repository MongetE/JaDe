1. I

2. They throw in Drummer Hodge, to rest
3. Uncoffined--just as found:
4. His landmark is a kopje-crest
5. That breaks the veldt around;
6. And foreign constellations west
7. Each night above his mound.

8. II

9. Young Hodge the Drummer never knew -
10. Fresh from his Wessex home -
11. The meaning of the broad Karoo,
12. The Bush, the dusty loam,
13. And why uprose to nightly view
14. Strange stars amid the gloam.

15. III

16. Yet portion of that unknown plain
17. Will Hodge for ever be;
18. His homely Northern breast and brain
19. Grow up a Southern tree.
20. And strange-eyed constellations reign
21. His stars eternally.
