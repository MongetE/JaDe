1. If grief for grief can touch thee,
2. If answering woe for woe,
3. If any truth can melt thee
4. Come to me now!

5. I cannot be more lonely,
6. More drear I cannot be!
7. My worn heart beats so wildly
8. 'Twill break for thee--

9. And when the world despises--
10. When Heaven repels my prayer--
11. Will not mine angel comfort?
12. Mine idol hear?

13. Yes, by the tears I'm poured,
14. By all my hours of pain
15. O I shall surely win thee,
16. Beloved, again!
