1. Like some rare queen of old romance
2. Who loved the gleam of helm and lance
3. Is she.
4. A harper of King Arthur's days
5. Should praise her in a hundred lays:
6. The queen of Love and Chivalry —
7. O Dieu te garde, mon coeur, ma vie.
8. And crown-wise plaited is her hair,
9. No crown of woven gold more fair
10. Could be.
11. And very queen-like, too, the smile
12. That lightens every little while
13. A face too fair for men to see,
14. O Dieu te garde, mon coeur, ma vie.
15. She is not over kind, I know;
16. The queens were gracious long ago,
17. Ah me!
18. Queen Guenevere would give a kiss
19. Ofttimes to Launcelot, I wis —
20. I would that I were loved as he!
21. O Dieu te garde, mon coeur, ma vie,
