1. Hope went by and Peace went by
2. And would not enter in;
3. Youth went by and Health wnt by
4. And Love that is their kin.

5. Those within the house shed tears
6. On their bitter bread;
7. Some were old and some were mad,
8. And some were sick a-bed.

9. Gray Death saw the wretched house
10. And even he passed by--
11. "They have never lived," he said,
12. "They can wait to die."
