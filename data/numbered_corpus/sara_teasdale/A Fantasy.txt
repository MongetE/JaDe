1. Her voice is like clear water
2. That drips upon a stone
3. In forests far and silent
4. Where Quiet plays alone.

5. Her thoughts are like the lotus
6. Abloom by sacred streams
7. Beneath the temple arches
8. Where Quiet sits and dreams.

9. Her kisses are the roses
10. That glow while dusk is deep
11. In Persian garden closes
12. Where Quiet falls asleep.
