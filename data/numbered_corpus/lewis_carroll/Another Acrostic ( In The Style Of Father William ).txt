1. "Are you deaf, Father William!" the young man said,
2. "Did you hear what I told you just now?
3. "Excuse me for shouting! Don't waggle your head
4. "Like a blundering, sleepy old cow!
5. "A little maid dwelling in Wallington Town,
6. "Is my friend, so I beg to remark:
7. "Do you think she'd be pleased if a book were sent down
8. "Entitled 'The Hunt of the Snark?'"


9. "Pack it up in brown paper!" the old man cried,
10. "And seal it with olive-and-dove.
11. "I command you to do it!" he added with pride,
12. "Nor forget, my good fellow to send her beside
13. "Easter Greetings, and give her my love."
