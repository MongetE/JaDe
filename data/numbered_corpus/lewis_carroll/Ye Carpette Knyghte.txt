1. I have a horse - a ryghte good horse -
2. Ne doe Y envye those
3. Who scoure ye playne yn headye course
4. Tyll soddayne on theyre nose
5. They lyghte wyth unexpected force
6. Yt ys - a horse of clothes.

7. I have a saddel - "Say'st thou soe?
8. Wyth styrruppes, Knyghte, to boote?"
9. I sayde not that - I answere "Noe" -
10. Yt lacketh such, I woote:
11. Yt ys a mutton-saddel, loe!
12. Parte of ye fleecye brute.

13. I have a bytte - a ryghte good bytte -
14. As shall bee seene yn tyme.
15. Ye jawe of horse yt wyll not fytte;
16. Yts use ys more sublyme.
17. Fayre Syr, how deemest thou of yt?
18. Yt ys - thys bytte of rhyme.
