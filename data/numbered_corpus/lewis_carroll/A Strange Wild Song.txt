1. He thought he saw an Elephant
2. That practised on a fife:
3. He looked again, and found it was
4. A letter from his wife.
5. 'At length I realize,' he said,
6. 'The bitterness of life! '

7. He thought he saw a Buffalo
8. Upon the chimney-piece:
9. He looked again, and found it was
10. His Sister's Husband's Niece.
11. 'Unless you leave this house,' he said,
12. 'I'll send for the police! '

13. he thought he saw a Rattlesnake
14. That questioned him in Greek:
15. He looked again, and found it was
16. The Middle of Next Week.
17. 'The one thing I regret,' he said,
18. 'Is that it cannot speak! '

19. He thought he saw a Banker's Clerk
20. Descending from the bus:
21. He looked again, and found it was
22. A Hippopotamus.
23. 'If this should stay to dine,' he said,
24. 'There won't be much for us! '

25. He thought he saw a Kangaroo
26. That worked a Coffee-mill:
27. He looked again, and found it was
28. A Vegetable-Pill.
29. 'Were I to swallow this,' he said,
30. 'I should be very ill! '

31. He thought he saw a Coach-and-Four
32. That stood beside his bed:
33. He looked again, and found it was
34. A Bear without a Head.
35. 'Poor thing,' he said, 'poor silly thing!
36. It's waiting to be fed! '

37. He thought he saw an Albatross
38. That fluttered round the lamp:
39. He looked again, and found it was
40. A Penny-Postag e Stamp.
41. 'You'd best be getting home,' he said:
42. 'The nights are very damp! '

43. He thought he saw a Garden-Door
44. That opened with a key:
45. He looked again, and found it was
46. A Double Rule of Three:
47. 'And all its mystery,' he said,
48. 'Is clear as day to me! '

49. He thought he saw a Argument
50. That proved he was the Pope:
51. He looked again, and found it was
52. A Bar of Mottled Soap.
53. 'A fact so dread,' he faintly said,
54. 'Extinguishe s all hope! '
