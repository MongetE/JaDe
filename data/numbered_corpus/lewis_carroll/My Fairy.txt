1. I have a fairy by my side
2. Which says I must not sleep,
3. When once in pain I loudly cried
4. It said "You must not weep"
5. If, full of mirth, I smile and grin,
6. It says "You must not laugh"
7. When once I wished to drink some gin
8. It said "You must not quaff".

9. When once a meal I wished to taste
10. It said "You must not bite"
11. When to the wars I went in haste
12. It said "You must not fight".

13. "What may I do?" at length I cried,
14. Tired of the painful task.
15. The fairy quietly replied,
16. And said "You must not ask".

17. Moral: "You mustn't."
