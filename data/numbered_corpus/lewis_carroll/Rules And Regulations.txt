1. A short direction
2. To avoid dejection,
3. By variations
4. In occupations,
5. And prolongation
6. Of relaxation,
7. And combinations
8. Of recreations,
9. And disputation
10. On the state of the nation
11. In adaptation
12. To your station,
13. By invitations
14. To friends and relations,
15. By evitation
16. Of amputation,
17. By permutation
18. In conversation,
19. And deep reflection
20. You'll avoid dejection.

21. Learn well your grammar,
22. And never stammer,
23. Write well and neatly,
24. And sing most sweetly,
25. Be enterprising,
26. Love early rising,
27. Go walk of six miles,
28. Have ready quick smiles,
29. With lightsome laughter,
30. Soft flowing after.
31. Drink tea, not coffee;
32. Never eat toffy.
33. Eat bread with butter.
34. Once more, don't stutter.

35. Don't waste your money,
36. Abstain from honey.
37. Shut doors behind you,
38. (Don't slam them, mind you.)
39. Drink beer, not porter.
40. Don't enter the water
41. Till to swim you are able.
42. Sit close to the table.
43. Take care of a candle.
44. Shut a door by the handle,
45. Don't push with your shoulder
46. Until you are older.
47. Lose not a button.
48. Refuse cold mutton.
49. Starve your canaries.
50. Believe in fairies.
51. If you are able,
52. Don't have a stable
53. With any mangers.
54. Be rude to strangers.

55. Moral: Behave.
