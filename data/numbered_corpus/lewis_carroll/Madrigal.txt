1. (To Miss May Forshall.)


2. HE shouts amain, he shouts again,
3. (Her brother, fierce, as bluff King Hal),
4. "I tell you flat, I shall do that!"
5. She softly whispers " 'May' for 'shall'!"
6. He wistful sighed one eventide
7. (Her friend, that made this Madrigal),
8. "And shall I kiss you, pretty Miss!"
9. Smiling she answered " 'May' for 'shall'!"

10. With eager eyes my reader cries,
11. "Your friend must be indeed a val-
12. -uable child, so sweet, so mild!
13. What do you call her?" "May For shall."
