1. WHO fancied what a pretty sight
2. This Rock would be if edged around
3. With living snow-drops? circlet bright!
4. How glorious to this orchard-ground!
5. Who loved the little Rock, and set
6. Upon its head this coronet?

7. Was it the humour of a child?
8. Or rather of some gentle maid,
9. Whose brows, the day that she was styled
10. The shepherd-queen, were thus arrayed?
11. Of man mature, or matron sage?
12. Or old man toying with his age!

13. I asked--'twas whispered; The device
14. To each and all might well belong:
15. It is the Spirit of Paradise
16. That prompts such work, a Spirit strong,
17. That gives to all the self-same bent
18. Where life is wise and innocent.
