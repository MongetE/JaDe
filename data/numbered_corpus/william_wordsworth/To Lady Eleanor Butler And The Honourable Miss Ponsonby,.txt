1. A stream to mingle with your favorite Dee
2. Along the Vale of Meditation flows;
3. So styled by those fierce Britons, pleased to see
4. In Nature's face the expression of repose,
5. Or, haply there some pious Hermit chose
6. To live and die -- the peace of Heaven his aim,
7. To whome the wild sequestered region owes
8. At this late day, its sanctifying name.
9. Glyn Cafaillgaroch, in the Cambrian tongue,
10. In ourse the Vale of Friendship, let this spot
11. Be nam'd, where faithful to a low roof'd Cot
12. On Deva's banks, ye have abode so long,
13. Sisters in love, a love allowed to climb
14. Ev'n on this earth, above the reach of time.
