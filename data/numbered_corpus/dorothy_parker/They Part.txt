1. And if, my friend, you'd have it end,
2. There's naught to hear or tell.
3. But need you try to black my eye
4. In wishing me farewell.

5. Though I admit an edged wit
6. In woe is warranted,
7. May I be frank? . . . Such words as "-"
8. Are better left unsaid.

9. There's rosemary for you and me;
10. But is it usual, dear,
11. To hire a man, and fill a van
12. By way of souvenir?
