1. ... So, praise the gods, Catullus is away!
2. And let me tend you this advice, my dear:
3. Take any lover that you will, or may,
4. Except a poet. All of them are queer.

5. It's just the same- a quarrel or a kiss
6. Is but a tune to play upon his pipe.
7. He's always hymning that or wailing this;
8. Myself, I much prefer the business type.

9. That thing he wrote, the time the sparrow died-
10. (Oh, most unpleasant- gloomy, tedious words!)
11. I called it sweet, and made believe I cried;
12. The stupid fool! I've always hated birds....
