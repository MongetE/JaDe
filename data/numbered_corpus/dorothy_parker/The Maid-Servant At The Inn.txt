1. "It's queer," she said; "I see the light
2. As plain as I beheld it then,
3. All silver-like and calm and bright-
4. We've not had stars like that again!

5. "And she was such a gentle thing
6. To birth a baby in the cold.
7. The barn was dark and frightening-
8. This new one's better than the old.

9. "I mind my eyes were full of tears,
10. For I was young, and quick distressed,
11. But she was less than me in years
12. That held a son against her breast.

13. "I never saw a sweeter child-
14. The little one, the darling one!-
15. I mind I told her, when he smiled
16. You'd know he was his mother's son.

17. "It's queer that I should see them so-
18. The time they came to Bethlehem
19. Was more than thirty years ago;
20. I've prayed that all is well with them."
