1. We are the children of light, 
2. Wise, not companioned
3. By goats
4. In a condemned graveyard.

5. Backward blowing
6. Blizzards of memory
7. Flatten out
8. The genealogies.

9. But here a point,
10. The objective essence
11. We work in.
12. We shall not drink from the stink-pots.

13. Propaganda,
14. Gospel spread
15. With tin shovels,
16. We are this generation.
