
1. I have lived in important places, times
2. When great events were decided, who owned
3. That half a rood of rock, a no-man's land
4. Surrounded by our pitchfork-armed claims.
5. I heard the Duffys shouting "Damn your soul!"
6. And old McCabe stripped to the waist, seen
7. Step the plot defying blue cast-steel -
8. "Here is the march along these iron stones."
9. That was the year of the Munich bother. Which
10. Was more important? I inclined
11. To lose my faith in Ballyrush and Gortin
12. Till Homer's ghost came whispering to my mind.
13. He said: I made the Iliad from such
14. A local row. Gods make their own importance.
