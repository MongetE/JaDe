1. deep in liquid
2. turquoise slivers
3. of dilute light

4. quiver in thin streaks
5. of bright tinfoil
6. on mobile jet:

7. pale flounder
8. waver by
9. tilting silver:

10. in the shallows
11. agile minnows
12. flicker gilt:

13. grapeblue mussels
14. dilate lithe and
15. pliant valves:

16. dull lunar globes
17. of blubous jellyfish
18. glow milkgreen:

19. eels twirl
20. in wily spirals
21. on elusive tails:

22. adroir lobsters
23. amble darkly olive
24. on shrewd claws:

25. down where sound
26. comes blunt and wan
27. like the bronze tone
28. of a sunken gong.
