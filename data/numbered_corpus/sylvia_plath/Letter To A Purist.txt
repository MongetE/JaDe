1. That grandiose colossus who
2. Stood astride
3. The envious assaults of sea
4. (Essaying, wave by wave,
5. Tide by tide,
6. To undo him, perpetually),
7. Has nothing on you,
8. O my love,
9. O my great idiot, who
10. With one foot
11. Caught (as it were) in the muck-trap
12. Of skin and bone,
13. Dithers with the other way out
14. In preposterous provinces of the madcap
15. Cloud-cuckoo,
16. Agawp at the impeccable moon.
