1. Some clichty folks
2. don't know the facts,
3. posin' and preenin'
4. and puttin' on acts,
5. stretchin' their backs.

6. They move into condos
7. up over the ranks,
8. pawn their souls
9. to the local banks.
10. Buying big cars
11. they can't afford,
12. ridin' around town
13. actin' bored.

14. If they want to learn how to live life right
15. they ought to study me on Saturday night.

16. My job at the plant
17. ain't the biggest bet,
18. but I pay my bills
19. and stay out of debt.
20. I get my hair done
21. for my own self's sake,
22. so I don't have to pick
23. and I don't have to rake.

24. Take the church money out
25. and head cross town
26. to my friend girl's house
27. where we plan our round.
28. We meet our men and go to a joint
29. where the music is blue
30. and to the point.

31. Folks write about me.
32. They just can't see
33. how I work all week
34. at the factory.
35. Then get spruced up
36. and laugh and dance
37. And turn away from worry
38. with sassy glance.

39. They accuse me of livin'
40. from day to day,
41. but who are they kiddin'?
42. So are they.

43. My life ain't heaven
44. but it sure ain't hell.
45. I'm not on top
46. but I call it swell
47. if I'm able to work
48. and get paid right
49. and have the luck to be Black
50. on a Saturday night.
