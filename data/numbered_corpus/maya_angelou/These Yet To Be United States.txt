1. Tremors of your network
2. cause kings to disappear.
3. Your open mouth in anger
4. makes nations bow in fear.

5. Your bombs can change the seasons,
6. obliterate the spring.
7. What more do you long for ?
8. Why are you suffering ?

9. You control the human lives
10. in Rome and Timbuktu.
11. Lonely nomads wandering
12. owe Telstar to you.

13. Seas shift at your bidding,
14. your mushrooms fill the sky.
15. Why are you unhappy ?
16. Why do your children cry ?

17. They kneel alone in terror
18. with dread in every glance.
19. Their nights ['rights' ? - Schrift nicht lesbar] are threatened daily
20. by a grim inheritance.

21. You dwell in whitened castles
22. with deep and poisoned moats
23. and cannot hear the curses
24. which fill your children's throats.
