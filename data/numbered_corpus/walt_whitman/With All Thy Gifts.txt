1. WITH all thy gifts, America,
2. (Standing secure, rapidly tending, overlooking the world,)
3. Power, wealth, extent, vouchsafed to thee--With these, and like of
4. these, vouchsafed to thee,
5. What if one gift thou lackest? (the ultimate human problem never
6. solving;)
7. The gift of Perfect Women fit for thee--What of that gift of gifts
8. thou lackest?
9. The towering Feminine of thee? the beauty, health, completion, fit
10. for thee?
11. The Mothers fit for thee?
