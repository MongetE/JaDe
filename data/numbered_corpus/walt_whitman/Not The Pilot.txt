1. NOT the pilot has charged himself to bring his ship into port, though
2. beaten back, and many times baffled;
3. Not the path-finder, penetrating inland, weary and long,
4. By deserts parch'd, snows-chill'd, rivers wet, perseveres till he
5. reaches his destination,
6. More than I have charged myself, heeded or unheeded, to compose a
7. free march for These States,
8. To be exhilarating music to them--a battle-call, rousing to arms, if
9. need be--years, centuries hence.
