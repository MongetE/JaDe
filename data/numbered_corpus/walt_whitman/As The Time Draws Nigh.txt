1. AS the time draws nigh, glooming, a cloud,
2. A dread beyond, of I know not what, darkens me.

3. I shall go forth,
4. I shall traverse The States awhile--but I cannot tell whither or how
5. long;
6. Perhaps soon, some day or night while I am singing, my voice will
7. suddenly cease.


8. O book, O chants! must all then amount to but this?
9. Must we barely arrive at this beginning of us?... And yet it is
10. enough, O soul!
11. O soul! we have positively appear'd--that is enough.
