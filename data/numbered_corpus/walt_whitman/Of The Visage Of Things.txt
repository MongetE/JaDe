1. OF the visages of things--And of piercing through to the accepted
2. hells beneath;
3. Of ugliness--To me there is just as much in it as there is in
4. beauty--And now the ugliness of human beings is acceptable to
5. me;
6. Of detected persons--To me, detected persons are not, in any respect,
7. worse than undetected persons--and are not in any respect worse
8. than I am myself;
9. Of criminals--To me, any judge, or any juror, is equally criminal--
10. and any reputable person is also--and the President is also.
