1. HOW they are provided for upon the earth, (appearing at intervals;)
2. How dear and dreadful they are to the earth;
3. How they inure to themselves as much as to any--What a paradox
4. appears their age;
5. How people respond to them, yet know them not;
6. How there is something relentless in their fate, all times;
7. How all times mischoose the objects of their adulation and reward,
8. And how the same inexorable price must still be paid for the same
9. great purchase.
