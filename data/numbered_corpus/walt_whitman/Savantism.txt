1. THITHER, as I look, I see each result and glory retracing itself and
2. nestling close, always obligated;
3. Thither hours, months, years--thither trades, compacts,
4. establishments, even the most minute;
5. Thither every-day life, speech, utensils, politics, persons, estates;
6. Thither we also, I with my leaves and songs, trustful, admirant,
7. As a father, to his father going, takes his children along with him.
