1. THICK-SPRINKLED bunting! Flag of stars!
2. Long yet your road, fateful flag!--long yet your road, and lined with
3. bloody death!
4. For the prize I see at issue, at last is the world!
5. All its ships and shores I see, interwoven with your threads, greedy
6. banner!
7. --Dream'd again the flags of kings, highest born, to flaunt
8. unrival'd?
9. O hasten, flag of man! O with sure and steady step, passing highest
10. flags of kings,
11. Walk supreme to the heavens, mighty symbol--run up above them all,
12. Flag of stars! thick-sprinkled bunting!
