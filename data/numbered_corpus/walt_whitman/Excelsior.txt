1. WHO has gone farthest? For lo! have not I gone farther?
2. And who has been just? For I would be the most just person of the
3. earth;
4. And who most cautious? For I would be more cautious;
5. And who has been happiest? O I think it is I! I think no one was ever
6. happier than I;
7. And who has lavish'd all? For I lavish constantly the best I have;
8. And who has been firmest? For I would be firmer;
9. And who proudest? For I think I have reason to be the proudest son
10. alive--for I am the son of the brawny and tall-topt city;
11. And who has been bold and true? For I would be the boldest and truest
12. being of the universe;
13. And who benevolent? For I would show more benevolence than all the
14. rest;
15. And who has projected beautiful words through the longest time? Have
16. I not outvied him? have I not said the words that shall stretch
17. through longer time?
18. And who has receiv'd the love of the most friends? For I know what it
19. is to receive the passionate love of many friends;
20. And who possesses a perfect and enamour'd body? For I do not believe
21. any one possesses a more perfect or enamour'd body than mine;
22. And who thinks the amplest thoughts? For I will surround those
23. thoughts;
24. And who has made hymns fit for the earth? For I am mad with devouring
25. extasy to make joyous hymns for the whole earth!
