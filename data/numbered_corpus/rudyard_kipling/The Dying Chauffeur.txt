1. Adam Lindsay Gordon


2. Wheel me gently to the garage, since my car and I must part--
3. No more for me the records and the run.
4. That cursed left-hand cylinder the doctors call my heart
5. Is pinking past redemption -- I am done!

6. They'll never strike a mixture that'll help me pull my load.
7. My gears are stripped--I cannot set my brakes.
8. I am entered for the finals down the timeless untimed Road
9. To the Maker of the makers of all makes!
