1. Praed


2. There was a landau deep and wide,
3. Cushioned for Sleep's own self to sit on--
4. The glory of the country-side
5. From Tanner's End to Marlow Ditton.
6. John of the broad and brandied cheek
7. (Well I recall its eau-de-vie hues!)
8. Drove staid Sir Ralph five days a week
9. At speeds which we considered Jehu's...

10. But now' poor John sleeps very sound,
11. And neither hears nor smells the fuss
12. Of the young Squire's nine-hundred-pound--
13. Er-Mors communis omnibus.
14. And I who in my daily stroll
15. Observe the reckless chauffeur crowd her,
16. Laudator temporis, extol
17. The times before the Act allowed her.
