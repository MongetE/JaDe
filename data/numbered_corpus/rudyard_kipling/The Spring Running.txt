1. Man goes to Man! Cry the challenge through the Jungle!
2. He that was our Brother goes away.
3. Hear, now, and judge, O ye People of the Jungle--
4. Answer, who can turn him--who shall stay?

5. Man goes to Man! He is weeping in the Jungle:
6. He that was our Brother sorrows sore!
7. Man goes to Man! (Oh, we loved him in the Jungle!)
8. To the Man-Trail where we may not follow more.
