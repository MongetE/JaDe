1. I

2. Frost upon small rain--the ebony-lacquered avenue
3. Reflecting lamps as a pool shows goldfish.
4. The sight suddenly emptied out of the young man's eyes
5. Entering upon it sideways.


6. II

7. In youth, by hazard, I killed an old man.
8. In age I maimed a little child.
9. Dead leaves under Foot reproach not:
10. But the lop-sided cherry-branch--whenever the sun rises,
11. How black a shadow!
