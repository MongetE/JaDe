1. "I have a thousand men," said he,
2. "To wait upon my will;
3. And towers nine upon the Tyne,
4. And three upon the Till."

5. "And what care I for your men? " said she,
6. "Or towers from Tyne to Till?
7. Sith you must go with me," said she,
8. "To wait upon my will.

9. And you may lead a thousand men
10. Nor ever draw the rein,
11. But before you lead the Fairy Queen
12. 'Twill burst your heart in twain."

13. He has slipped his foot from the stirrup-bar,
14. The bridle from his hand,
15. And he is bound by hand and foot
16. To the Queen of Fairy Land.
