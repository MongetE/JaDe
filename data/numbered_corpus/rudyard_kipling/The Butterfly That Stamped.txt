1. There was never a Queen like Balkis,
2. From here to the wide world's end;
3. But Balkis talked to a butterfly
4. As you would talk to a friend.

5. There was never a King like Solomon
6. Not since the world began;
7. But Solomon talked to a butterfly
8. As a man would talk to a man.

9. She was Queen of Sabea--
10. And he was Asia's Lord--
11. But they both of 'em talked to butterflies
12. When they took their walks abroad!
