1. Whether to wend through straight streets strictly,
2. Trimly by towns perfectly paved;
3. Or after office, as fitteth thy fancy,
4. Faring with friends far among fields;
5. There is none other equal in action,
6. Sith she  is silent,  nimble,  unnoisome,
7. Lordly of leather, gaudily gilded,
8. Burgeoning brightly in a brass bonnet,
9. Certain to steer well between wains.
