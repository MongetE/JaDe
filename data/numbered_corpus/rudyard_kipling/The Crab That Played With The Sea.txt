1. China-going P. & O.'s
2. Pass Pau Amma's playground close,
3. And his Pusat Tasek lies
4. Near the track of most B.I.'s.
5. N.Y.K. and N.D.L.
6. Know Pau Amma's home as well
7. As the Fisher of the Sea knows
8. "Bens," M.M.'s and Rubattinos.
9. But (and this is rather queer)
10. A.T.L.'s can qiot come here;
11. O. and O. and D.O.A.
12. Must go round another way.
13. Orient, Anchor, Bibby, Hall,
14. Never go that way at all.
15. U.C.S. would have a fit
16. If it found itself on it.
17. And if "Beavers" took their cargoes
18. To Penang instead of Lagos,
19. Or a fat Shaw-Savill bore
20. Passengers to Singapore,
21. Or a White Star were to try a
22. Little trip to Sourabaya,
23. Or a B.S.A. went on
24. Past Natal to Cheribon,
25. The the great Mr. Lloyds would come
26. Whith a wire and drag them home!
