1. "I WANT a chance to show what I can do,'
2. He sighed when others seemed to pass him by;
3. 'There are great problems I could master, too,
4. Somehow, I never get the chance to try.

5. Give me a chance to show what I can do,'
6. This was the burden of his daily whine;
7. 'I might achieve success as well as you,
8. If opportunity were mine.'

9. One day they bade him fill another's place,
10. Another's work they offered him to do;
11. He grumbled and a frown passed o'er his face,
12. 'I am not paid to do the work of two.'
