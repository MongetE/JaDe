1. DERE never was a man on earth
2. So wonderful or clever,
3. Dat ever found a way t' live
4. On dis ole world forever.

5. Dere never was a man so rich,
6. Dat didn't have t' go
7. When ole man Death came after him
8. An' crooked his finger, so.

9. An' den dere never was a man
10. So great, when he was gone
11. But what dis good ole world of ours
12. Jes' kep' a-waggin' on.

13. An' since dis ole world never stops
14. When famous men depart,
15. I' ve come t' de conclusion dat
16. We ain't so awful smart.
