1. My name is Johnny Vincent Brown,
2. I live on Leicester Court,
3. My Pa's not here, he's gone downtown,
4. An' I am three feet short,
5. An' I weigh sixty-three pounds, too,
6. An' I know my A, B, C's,
7. An' I say good-bye an' howdy-do,
8. An' yessum, yessir, please.

9. An' I'm a good boy all the time,
10. I do jes' what I'm told,
11. I like ter run an' jump an' climb,
12. I'm only four years old.
13. I don't like hair that hangs in curls,
14. An' I am fond of cake,
15. But I ain't got no use for girls
16. An' I hate stummick ache.

17. I like the baby that we've got,
18. I go ter Sunday School,
19. I say my prayers beside my cot,
20. I know the Golden Rule.
21. I'll be a man when I grow up,
22. I've got a dandy sleigh,
23. An' if that's all you want ter know
24. I'll skip along an' play.
