1. There is something about a Martini,
2. A tingle remarkably pleasant;
3. A yellow, a mellow Martini;
4. I wish I had one at present.
5. There is something about a Martini,
6. Ere the dining and dancing begin,
7. And to tell you the truth,
8. It is not the vermouth--
9. I think that perhaps it's the gin.
