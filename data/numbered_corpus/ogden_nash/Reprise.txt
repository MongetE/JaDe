1. Geniuses of countless nations
2. Have told their love for generations
3. Till all their memorable phrases
4. Are common as goldenrod or daisies.
5. Their girls have glimmered like the moon,
6. Or shimmered like a summer moon,
7. Stood like a lily, fled like a fawn,
8. Now the sunset, now the dawn,
9. Here the princess in the tower
10. There the sweet forbidden flower.
11. Darling, when I look at you
12. Every aged phrase is new,
13. And there are moments when it seems
14. I've married one of Shakespeare's dreams.
