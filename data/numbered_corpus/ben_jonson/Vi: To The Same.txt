1. Kisse mee, Sweet: The wary lover
2. Can your favours keepe, and cover,
3. When the common courting jay
4. All your bounties will betray.
5. Kisse againe: no creature comes.
6. Kisse, and score up wealthy summes
7. On my lips, thus hardly sundred,
8. While you breathe. First give a hundred,
9. Then a thousand, then another
10. Hundred, then unto the tother
11. Adde a thousand, and so more:
12. Till you equall with the store,
13. All the grasse that Rumney yeelds,
14. Or the sands in Chelsey fields,
15. Or the drops in silver Thames,
16. Or the stars, that guild his streames,
17. In the silent sommer-nights,
18. When youths ply their stoln delights.
19. That the curious may not know
20. How to tell 'hem as they flow,
21. And the envious, when they find
22. What their number is, be pin'd.
