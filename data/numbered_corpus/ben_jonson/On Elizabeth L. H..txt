1. Epitaphs i

2. WOULDST thou hear what Man can say
3. In a little? Reader, stay.
4. Underneath this stone doth lie
5. As much Beauty as could die:
6. Which in life did harbour give
7. To more Virtue than doth live.
8. If at all she had a fault,
9. Leave it buried in this vault.
10. One name was Elizabeth,
11. The other, let it sleep with death:
12. Fitter, where it died, to tell
13. Than that it lived at all. Farewell.
