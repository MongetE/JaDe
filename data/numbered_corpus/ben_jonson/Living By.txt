1. Walking, snow falling, it is possible
2. to focus at various distances
3. in turn on separate flakes, sharply engage
4. the attention at several spatial points:
5. the nearer cold and more uncomfortable,
6. the farther distanced and almost pleasing.

7. Living, time passing, it is preferable
8. to focus the memory in turn upon
9. the more distant retrospects in order
10. that the present mind may retain its peace.

11. Yet knowing that seeing and remembering
12. are both of course personal illusions.
