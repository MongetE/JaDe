1. if only for ten minutes

2. after the mass feeding of schoolchildren
3. after the careful inanity of the staff

4. at low tide
5. this was the place
6. for calm, for order of a kind

7. the relief of walking there

8. and the smell was acceptable
9. perhaps even preferable

10. the objects to be
11. seen
12. found

13. principally (I have it still)
14. a short fat halfpound brass bolt and nut
15. virgin, unscrewed

16. other things less permanent

17. sodden grey bones
18. scratched glass, rubbed brick, rusted gatebutts
19. once a chaffinch eggshell

20. every conceivable other

21. but mainly dirty shingle
22. silt
23. prairies of malachite slime

24. though was the important thing
25. that I met no one else there?
