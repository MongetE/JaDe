1. O YONGE fresshe folkes, he or she,
2. In which that love up groweth with your age,
3. Repeyreth hoom from worldly vanitee,
4. And of your herte up-casteth the visage
5. To thilke god that after his image
6. Yow made, and thinketh al nis but a fayre
7. This world, that passeth sone as floures fayre.

8. And loveth him, the which that right for love
9. Upon a cros, our soules for to beye,
10. First starf, and roos, and sit in hevene a-bove;
11. For he nil falsen no wight, dar I seye,
12. That wol his herte al hoolly on him leye.
13. And sin he best to love is, and most meke,
14. What nedeth feyned loves for to seke?
