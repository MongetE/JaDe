1. To say I'm without fear--
2. It wouldn't be true.
3. I'm afraid of sickness, humiliation.
4. Like anyone, I have my dreams.
5. But I've learned to hide them,
6. To protect myself
7. From fulfillment: all happiness
8. Attracts the Fates' anger.
9. They are sisters, savages--
10. In the end they have
11. No emotion but envy.
