1. Let the snake wait under
2. his weed
3. and the writing
4. be of words, slow and quick, sharp
5. to strike, quiet to wait,
6. sleepless.
7. -- through metaphor to reconcile
8. the people and the stones.
9. Compose. (No ideas
10. but in things) Invent!
11. Saxifrage is my flower that splits
12. the rocks.
