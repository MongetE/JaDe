1. First he said:
2. It is the woman in us
3. That makes us write-
4. Let us acknowledge it-
5. Men would be silent.
6. We are not men
7. Therefore we can speak
8. And be conscious
9. (of the two sides)
10. Unbent by the sensual
11. As befits accuracy.

12. I then said:
13. Dare you make this
14. Your propaganda?

15. And he answered:
16. Am I not I-here?
