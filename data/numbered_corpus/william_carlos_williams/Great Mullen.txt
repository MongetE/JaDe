1. One leaves his leaves at home
2. beomg a mullen and sends up a lighthouse
3. to peer from: I will have my way,
4. yellow--A mast with a lantern, ten
5. fifty, a hundred, smaller and smaller
6. as they grow more--Liar, liar, liar!
7. You come from her! I can smell djer-kiss
8. on your clothes. Ha! you come to me,
9. you, I am a point of dew on a grass-stem.
10. Why are you sending heat down on me
11. from your lantern?--You are cowdung, a
12. dead stick with the bark off. She is
13. squirting on us both. She has has her
14. hand on you!--well?--She has defiled
15. ME.--Your leaves are dull, thick
16. and hairy.--Every hair on my body will
17. hold you off from me. You are a
18. dungcake, birdlime on a fencerail.--
19. I love you, straight, yellow
20. finger of God pointing to--her!
21. Liar, broken weed, dungcake, you have--
22. I am a cricket waving his antennae
23. and you are high, grey and straight. Ha!
