1. The Archer is wake!
2. The Swan is flying!
3. Gold against blue
4. An Arrow is lying.
5. There is hunting in heaven--
6. Sleep safe till tomorrow.
7. The Bears are abroad!
8. The Eagle is screaming!
9. Gold against blue
10. Their eyes are gleaming!
11. Sleep!
12. Sleep safe till tomorrow.
13. The Sisters lie
14. With their arms intertwining;
15. Gold against blue
16. Their hair is shining!
17. The Serpent writhes!
18. Orion is listening!
19. Gold against blue
20. His sword is glistening!
21. Sleep!
22. There is hunting in heaven--
23. Sleep safe till tomorrow.
