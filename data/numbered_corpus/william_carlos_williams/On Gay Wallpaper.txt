1. The green-blue ground
2. is ruled with silver lines
3. to say the sun is shining

4. And on this moral sea
5. of grass or dreams lie flowers
6. or baskets of desires

7. Heaven knows what they are
8. between cerulean shapes
9. laid regularly round

10. Mat roses and tridentate
11. leaves of gold
12. threes, threes and threes

13. Three roses and three stems
14. the basket floating
15. standing in the horns of blue

16. Repeating to the ceiling
17. to the windows
18. where the day

19. Blows in
20. the scalloped curtains to
21. the sound of rain
