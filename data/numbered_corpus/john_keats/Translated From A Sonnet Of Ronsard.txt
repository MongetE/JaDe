1. Nature withheld Cassandra in the skies
2. For more adornment a full thousand years;
3. She took their cream of Beauty's fairest dyes,
4. And shap'd and tinted her above all Peers:
5. Meanwhile Love kept her dearly with his wings,
6. And underneath their shadow fill'd her eyes
7. With such a richness that the cloudy Kings
8. Of high Olympus utter'd slavish sighs.
9. When from the Heavens I saw her first descend
10. My heart took fire, and only burning pains
11. They were my pleasures -- they my Life's sad end;
12. Love pour'd her beauty into my warm veins...
