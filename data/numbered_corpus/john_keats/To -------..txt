1. 1.
2. Think not of it, sweet one, so;--
3. Give it not a tear;
4. Sigh thou mayst, and bid it go
5. Any, any where.

6. 2.
7. Do not look so sad, sweet one,--
8. Sad and fadingly;
9. Shed one drop then, it is gone,
10. O 'twas born to die!

11. 3.
12. Still so pale? then, dearest, weep;
13. Weep, I'll count the tears,
14. And each one shall be a bliss
15. For thee in after years.

16. 4.
17. Brighter has it left thine eyes
18. Than a sunny rill;
19. And thy whispering melodies
20. Are tenderer still.

21. 5.
22. Yet -- as all things mourn awhile
23. At fleeting blisses,
24. E'en let us too! but be our dirge
25. A dirge of kisses.
