1. BEFORE he went to feed with owls and bats
2. Nebuchadnezzar had an ugly dream,
3. Worse than an Hus'if's when she thinks her cream
4. Made a Naumachia for mice and rats.
5. So scared, he sent for that 'Good King of Cats'
6. Young Daniel, who soon did pluck away the beam
7. From out his eye, and said he did not deem
8. The sceptre worth a straw his Cushions old door-mats.
9. A horrid nightmare similar somewhat
10. Of late has haunted a most motley crew,
11. Most loggerheads and Chapmen we are told
12. That any Daniel tho' he be a sot
13. Can make the lying lips turn pale of hue
14. By belching out 'ye are that head of Gold.'
