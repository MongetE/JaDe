1. HITHER hither, love---
2. 'Tis a shady mead---
3. Hither, hither, love!
4. Let us feed and feed!

5. Hither, hither, sweet---
6. 'Tis a cowslip bed---
7. Hither, hither, sweet!
8. 'Tis with dew bespread!

9. Hither, hither, dear
10. By the breath of life,
11. Hither, hither, dear!---
12. Be the summer's wife!

13. Though one moment's pleasure
14. In one moment flies---
15. Though the passion's treasure
16. In one moment dies;---

17. Yet it has not passed---
18. Think how near, how near!---
19. And while it doth last,
20. Think how dear, how dear!

21. Hither, hither, hither
22. Love its boon has sent---
23. If I die and wither
24. I shall die content!
