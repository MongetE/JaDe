1. Minnow, go to sleep and dream,
2. Close your great big eyes;
3. Round your bed Events prepare
4. The pleasantest surprise.

5. Darling Minnow, drop that frown,
6. Just cooperate,
7. Not a kitten shall be drowned
8. In the Marxist State.

9. Joy and Love will both be yours,
10. Minnow, don't be glum.
11. Happy days are coming soon --
12. Sleep, and let them come...
