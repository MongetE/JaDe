1. Caught -- the bubble
2. in the spirit level,
3. a creature divided;
4. and the compass needle
5. wobbling and wavering,
6. undecided.
7. Freed -- the broken
8. thermometer's mercury
9. running away;
10. and the rainbow-bird
11. from the narrow bevel
12. of the empty mirror,
13. flying wherever
14. it feels like, gay!
