1. These fought in any case,
2. and some believing
3. pro domo, in any case .....

4. Died some, pro patria,
5. walked eye-deep in hell
6. believing in old men's lies, then unbelieving
7. came home, home to a lie,
8. home to many deceits,
9. home to old lies and new infamy;
10. usury age-old and age-thick
11. and liars in public places.

12. Daring as never before, wastage as never before.
13. Young blood and high blood,
14. fair cheeks, and fine bodies;

15. fortitude as never before

16. frankness as never before,
17. disillusions as never told in the old days,
18. hysterias, trench confessions,
19. laughter out of dead bellies.
