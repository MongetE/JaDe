1. The gilded phaloi of the crocuses
2. are thrusting at the spring air.
3. Here is there naught of dead gods
4. But a procession of festival,
5. A procession, Giulio Romano,
6. Fit for your spirit to dwell in.
7. Dione, your nights are upon us.

8. The dew is upon the leaf.
9. The night about us is restless.
