1. When I was only a youngster,
2. Sing: toodle doodlede ootl
3. Ole Kate would git her 'arf a pint
4. And wouldn't' giv' a damn hoot.

5. 'Them stairs! them stairs, them gordam stairs
6. Will be the death of me/
7. I never heerd her say nothin'
8. About the priv'lege of liberty.

9. She'd come a sweatin' up with the coals
10. An a-sloshin' round with 'er mop,
11. Startin' in about 6 a.m.
12. And didn't seem never to stop.

13. She died on the job they tells me,
14. Fell plump into her pail.
15. Never got properly tanked as I saw,
16. And never got took to jail,

17. Just went on a sloshin'
18. And totin' up scuttles of coal,
19. And kissin9 her cat fer diversion,
20. Cod rest her sloshin’ soul.

21. ‘Gimme a kissy-cuddle'
22. She'd say to her tibby-cat,
23. But she never made no mention
24. Of this here proletariat.
