1. On the loan exhibit of his paintings at the Tate Gallery.

2. You also, our first great,
3. Had tried all ways;
4. Tested and pried and worked in many fashions,
5. And this much gives me heart to play the game.

6. Here is part that's slight, and part gone wrong,
7. And much of little moment, and some few
8. Perfect as Diirer!
9. 'In the Studio' and these two portraits, if I had my choice!
10. And then these sketches in the mood of Greece?

11. You had your searches, your uncertainties,
12. And this is good to know for us, I mean,
13. Who bear the brunt of our America
14. And try to wrench her impulse into art.

15. You were not always sure, not always set
16. To hiding night or tuning ^symphonies';
17. Had not one style from birth, but tried and pried
18. And stretched and tampered with the media.

19. You and Abe Lincoln from that mass of dolts
20. Show us there's chance at least of winning through.
