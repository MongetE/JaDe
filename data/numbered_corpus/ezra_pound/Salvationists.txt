1. I
2. Come, my songs, let us speak of perfection
3. We shall get ourselves rather disliked.

4. II
5. Ah yes, my songs, let us resurrect
6. The very excellent term Rusticus.
7. Let us apply it in all its opprobrium
8. To those to whom it applies.
9. And you may decline to make them immortal,
10. For we shall consider them and their state
11. In delicate
12. Opulent silence.

13. III
14. Come, my songs,
15. Let us take arms against this sea of stupidities-
16. Beginning with Mumpodorus;
17. And against this sea of vulgarities
18. Beginning with Nimmim;
19. And against this sea of imbeciles
20. All the Bulmenian literati.
