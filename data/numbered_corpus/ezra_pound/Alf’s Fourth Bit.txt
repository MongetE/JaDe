1. Rudyard the dud yard,
2. Rudyard the false measure,
3. Told 'em that glory
4. Ain't always a pleasure,
5. But said it wuz glorious nevertheless
6. To lick the boots of the bloke
7. That makes the worst mess.

8. Keep up the grand system
9. Don't tell what you know,
10. Your grandad got the rough edge.
11. Ain't it always been so ?
12. Your own ma' warn't no better
13. Than the Duchess of Kaugh.
14. My cousin's named Baldwin
15. An' 'e looks like a tofft

16. You 'ark to the sargent,
17. And don't read no books;
18. Go to God like a sojer;
19. What counts is the looks.
