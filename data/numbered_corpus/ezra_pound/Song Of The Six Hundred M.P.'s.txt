1. ‘We are 'ere met together
2. in this momentous hower,
3. Ter lick th' bankers' dirty boots
4. an' keep the Bank in power.’

5. We are 'ere met together
6. ter grind the same old axes
7. And keep the people in its place
8. a'payin' us the taxes.

9. We are six hundred beefy men
10. (but mostly gas and suet)
11. An’ every year we meet to let
12. some other feller do it.'

13. I see their 'igh 'ats on the seats
14. an' them sprawling on the benches
15. And thinks about a Rowton 'ouse
16. and a lot of small street stenches.

17. 'O Britain, muvver of parliaments,
18. 'ave you seen yer larst sweet litter?
19. Could yeh swap th' brains of orl this lot
20. fer 'arft a pint o' bitter?'

21. ‘I couldn't,' she sez, ‘an' I aint tried,
22. They're me own,' she sez to me,
23. ‘As footlin' a lot as was ever spawned
24. to defend democracy.'
