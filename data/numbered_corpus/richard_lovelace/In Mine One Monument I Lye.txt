1. I.

2. In mine one monument I lye,
3. And in my self am buried;
4. Sure, the quick lightning of her eye
5. Melted my soul ith' scabberd dead;
6. And now like some pale ghost I walk,
7. And with another's spirit talk.

8. II.

9. Nor can her beams a heat convey,
10. That may my frozen bosome warm,
11. Unless her smiles have pow'r, as they,
12. That a cross charm can countercharm.
13. But this is such a pleasing pain,
14. I'm loth to be alive again.
