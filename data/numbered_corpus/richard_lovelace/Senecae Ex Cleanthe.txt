1. SENECAE EX CLEANTHE.

2. Duc me, Parens celsique Dominator poli,
3. Quocunque placuit, nulla parendi mora est;
4. Adsum impiger; fac nolle, comitabor gemens,
5. Malusque patiar facere, quod licuit bono.
6. Ducunt volentem Fata, nolentem trahunt.

7. ENGLISHED.

8. Parent and Prince of Heav'n, O lead, I pray,
9. Where ere you please, I follow and obey.
10. Active I go, sighing, if you gainsay,
11. And suffer bad what to the good was law.
12. Fates lead the willing, but unwilling draw.
