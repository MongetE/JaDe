1. AUSONIUS LIB. I. EPIG.

2. Thesauro invento qui limina mortis inibat,
3. Liquit ovans laqueum, quo periturus erat;
4. At qui, quod terrae abdiderat, non repperit aurum,
5. Quem laqueum invenit nexuit, et periit.

6. ENGLISHED.

7. A treasure found one, entring at death's gate,
8. Triumphing leaves that cord, was meant his fate;
9. But he the gold missing, which he did hide,
10. The halter which he found he knit: so dy'd.
