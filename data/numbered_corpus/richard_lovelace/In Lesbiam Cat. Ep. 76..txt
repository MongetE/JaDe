1. IN LESBIAM CAT. EP. 76.

2. Huc est mens deducta tua, mea Lesbia, culpa,
3. Atque ita se officio perdidit ipsa suo.
4. Ut jam nec bene velle queam tibi, si optima sias:
5. Nec desistere amare, omnia si facias.

6. ENGLISHED.

7. By thy fault is my mind brought to that pass,
8. That it its office quite forgotten has:
9. For be'est thou best, I cannot wish thee well,
10. And be'est thou worst, then I must love thee still.
