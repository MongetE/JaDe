1. MART. EP. XV. LIB. 6.

2. Dum Phaetontea formica vagatur in umbra,
3. Implicuit tenuem succina gutta feram,
4. Dignum tantorum pretium tulit illa laborum:
5. Credibile est ipsam sic voluisse mori.

6. ENGLISHED.

7. Whilst in an amber-shade the ant doth feast,
8. A gummy drop ensnares the small wild-beast,
9. A full reward of all her toyls hath she;
10. 'Tis to be thought she would her self so die.
