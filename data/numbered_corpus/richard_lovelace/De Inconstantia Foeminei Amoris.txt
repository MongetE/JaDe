1. CATUL. EP. 71.

2. DE INCONSTANTIA FOEMINEI AMORIS.

3. Nulli se dicit mulier mea nubere velle,
4. Quam mihi: non, si Jupiter ipse petat;
5. Dicit; sed mulier cupido quod dicit amanti,
6. In vento et rapida scribere oportet aqua.

7. FEMALE INCONSTANCY.

8. My mistresse sayes she'll marry none but me;
9. No, not if Jove himself a suitor be.
10. She sayes so; but what women say to kind
11. Lovers, we write in rapid streams and wind.
