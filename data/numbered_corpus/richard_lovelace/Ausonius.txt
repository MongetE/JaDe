1. AUSONIUS.

2. Vane, quid affectas faciem mihi ponere, pictor,
3. Ignotamque oculis solicitare manu?
4. Aeris et venti sum filia, mater inanis
5. Indicii, vocemque sine mente gero.
6. Auribus in vestris habito penetrabilis echo;
7. Si mihi vis similem pingere, pinge sonos.

8. IN ENGLISH.

9. Vain painter, why dost strive my face to draw
10. With busy hands? a goddesse eyes nere saw.
11. Daughter of air and wind, I do rejoyce
12. In empty shouts; (without a mind) a voice.
13. Within your ears shrill echo I rebound,
14. And, if you'l paint me like, then paint a sound.
