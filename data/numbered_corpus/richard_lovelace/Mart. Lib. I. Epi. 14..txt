1. MART. LIB. I. EPI. 14.

2. Casta suo gladium cum traderet Arria Paeto,
3. Quem de visceribus traxerat ipsa suis;
4. Si qua fides, vulnus quod feci non dolet, inquit:
5. Sed quod tu facies, hoc mihi, Paete, dolet.

6. ENGLISHED.

7. When brave chast Arria to her Poetus gave
8. The sword from her own breast did bleeding wave:
9. If there be faith, this wound smarts not, said she;
10. But what you'l make, ah, that will murder me.
