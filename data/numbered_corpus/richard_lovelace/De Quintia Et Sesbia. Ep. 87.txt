1. DE QUINTIA ET LESBIA.  EP. 87.

2. Quintia formosa est multis, mihi candida, longa,
3. Recta est; haec ego sic singula confiteor:
4. Tota illud formosa nego: nam multa venustas;
5. Nulla in tam magno est corpore mica salis.
6. Lesbia formosa est quae, cum pulcherrima tota est,
7. Tum omnibus una omneis surripuit veneres.

8. ENGLISHED.

9. Quintia is handsome, fair, tall, straight: all these
10. Very particulars I grant with ease:
11. But she all ore 's not handsome; here's her fault:
12. In all that bulk there's not one corne of salt,
13. Whilst Lesbia, fair and handsome too all ore,
14. All graces and all wit from all hath bore.
