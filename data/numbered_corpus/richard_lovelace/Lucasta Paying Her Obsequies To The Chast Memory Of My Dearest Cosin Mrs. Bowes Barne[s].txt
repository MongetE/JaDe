1. I.
2. See! what an undisturbed teare
3. She weepes for her last sleepe;
4. But, viewing her, straight wak'd a Star,
5. She weepes that she did weepe.

6. II.
7. Griefe ne're before did tyranize
8. On th' honour of that brow,
9. And at the wheeles of her brave eyes
10. Was captive led til now.

11. III.
12. Thus, for a saints apostacy
13. The unimagin'd woes
14. And sorrowes of the Hierarchy
15. None but an angel knowes.

16. IV.
17. Thus, for lost soules recovery
18. The clapping of all wings
19. And triumphs of this victory
20. None but an angel sings.

21. V.
22. So none but she knows to bemone
23. This equal virgins fate,
24. None but LUCASTA can her crowne
25. Of glory celebrate.

26. VI.
27. Then dart on me (CHAST LIGHT) one ray,
28. By which I may discry
29. Thy joy cleare through this cloudy day
30. To dresse my sorrow by.
