1. I.
2. Lucasta wept, and still the bright
3. Inamour'd god of day,
4. With his soft handkercher of light,
5. Kist the wet pearles away.

6. II.
7. But when her teares his heate or'ecame,
8. In cloudes he quensht his beames,
9. And griev'd, wept out his eye of flame,
10. So drowned her sad streames.

11. III.
12. At this she smiled, when straight the sun
13. Cleer'd by her kinde desires;
14. And by her eyes reflexion
15. Fast kindl'd there his fires.
