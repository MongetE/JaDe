1. AVIENI V. C. AD AMICOS.

2. Rure morans, quid agam, respondi, pauca rogatus:
3. Mane, deum exoro famulos, post arvaque viso,
4. Partitusque meis justos indico labores;
5. Inde lego, Phoebumque cio, Musamque lacesso;
6. Tunc oleo corpus fingo, mollique palaestra
7. Stringo libens animo, gaudensque ac foenore liber
8. Prandeo, poto, cano, ludo, lavo, caeno, quiesco.

9. ENGLISHED.

10. Ask'd in the country what I did, I said:
11. I view my men and meads, first having pray'd;
12. Then each of mine hath his just task outlay'd;
13. I read, Apollo court, I rouse my Muse;
14. Then I anoynt me, and stript willing loose
15. My self on a soft plat, from us'ry blest;
16. I dine, drink, sing, play, bath, I sup, I rest.
