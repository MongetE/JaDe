1. SANAZARI HEXASTICON.

2. Viderat Adriacis quondam Neptunus in undis
3. Stare urbem et toto ponere Jura mari:
4. Nunc mihi Tarpeias quantumvis, Jupiter, Arces
5. Objice et illa mihi moenia Martis, ait,
6. Seu pelago Tibrim praefers, urbem aspice utramque,
7. Illam homines dices, hanc posuisse deos.

8. SANAZAR'S HEXASTICK.

9. In Adriatick waves when Neptune saw,
10. The city stand, and give the seas a law:
11. Now i' th' Tarpeian tow'rs Jove rival me,
12. And Mars his walls impregnable, said he;
13. Let seas to Tyber yield; view both their ods!
14. You'l grant that built by men, but this by gods.
