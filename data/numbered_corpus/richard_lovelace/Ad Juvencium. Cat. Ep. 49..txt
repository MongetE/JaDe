1. AD JUVENCIUM.  CAT. EP. 49.

2. Mellitos oculos tuos, Juvenci,
3. Si quis me sinat usque basiare,
4. Usque ad millia basiem trecenta;
5. Nec unquam videat satur futurus:
6. Non si densior aridis aristis,
7. Sit nostrae seges osculationis.

8. TO JUVENCIUS.

9. Juvencius, thy fair sweet eyes
10. If to my fill that I may kisse,
11. Three hundred thousand times I'de kisse,
12. Nor future age should cloy this blisse;
13. No, not if thicker than ripe ears
14. The harvest of our kisses bears.
