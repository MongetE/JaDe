1. Sir, how unravell'd is the golden fleece:
2. Men, that could only fool at FOX AND GEESE,
3. Are new-made polititians by thy book,
4. And both can judge and conquer with a look.
5. The hidden fate of princes you unfold;
6. Court, clergy, commons, by your law control'd.
7. Strange, serious wantoning all that they
8. Bluster'd and clutter'd for, you PLAY.
