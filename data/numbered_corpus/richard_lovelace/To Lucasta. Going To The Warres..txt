1. I.
2. Tell me not, (sweet,) I am unkinde,
3. That from the nunnerie
4. Of thy chaste breast and quiet minde
5. To warre and armes I flie.

6. II.
7. True: a new Mistresse now I chase,
8. The first foe in the field;
9. And with a stronger faith imbrace
10. A sword, a horse, a shield.

11. III.
12. Yet this inconstancy is such,
13. As you too shall adore;
14. I could not love thee, dear, so much,
15. Lov'd I not Honour more.
