1. AUSONIUS LIB. EPIG.

2. Trinarii quodam currentem in littoris ora
3. Ante canes leporem caeruleus rapuit;
4. At lepus: in me omnis terrae pelagique rapina est,
5. Forsitan et coeli, si canis astra tenet.

6. ENGLISHED.

7. On the Sicilian strand a hare well wrought
8. Before the hounds was by a dog-fish caught;
9. Quoth she: all rape of sea and earth's on me,
10. Perhaps of heav'n, if there a dog-star be.
