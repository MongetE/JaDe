1. ITEM.

2. Ictu non potuit primo Cato solvere vitam;
3. Defecit tanto vulnere victa manus:
4. Altius inseruit digitos, qua spiritus ingens
5. Exiret, magnum dextera fecit iter.
6. Opposuit fortuna moram, involvitque, Catonis
7. Scires ut ferro plus valuisse manum.

8. ANOTHER.

9. One stabbe could not fierce Cato's life unty;
10. Onely his hand of all that wound did dy.
11. Deeper his fingers tear to make a way
12. Open, through which his mighty soul might stray.
13. Fortune made this delay to let us know,
14. That Cato's hand more then his sword could do.
