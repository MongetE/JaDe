1. AUSONIUS EPIG.

2. Emptis quod libris tibi bibliotheca referta est,
3. Doctum et grammaticum te, philomuse, putas.
4. Quinetiam cytharas, chordas et barbita conde:
5. Mercator hodie, cras citharoedus, eris.

6. IN ENGLISH.

7. Because with bought books, sir, your study's fraught,
8. A learned grammarian you would fain be thought;
9. Nay then, buy lutes and strings; so you may play
10. The merchant now, the fidler, the next day.
