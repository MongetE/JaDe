1. I.

2. Depose your finger of that ring,
3. And crowne mine with't awhile;
4. Now I restor't. Pray, dos it bring
5. Back with it more of soile?
6. Or shines it not as innocent,
7. As honest, as before 'twas lent?

8. II.

9. So then inrich me with that treasure,
10. 'Twill but increase your store,
11. And please me (faire one) with that pleasure
12. Must please you still the more.
13. Not to save others is a curse
14. The blackest, when y'are ne're the worse.
