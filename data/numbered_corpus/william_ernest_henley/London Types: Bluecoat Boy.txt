1. So went our boys when Edward Sixth, the King,
2. Chartered Christ's Hospital, and died. And so
3. Full fifteen generations in a string
4. Of heirs to his bequest have had to go.
5. Thus Camden showed, and Barnes, and Stillingfleet,
6. And Richardson, that bade our Lovelace be;
7. Thus to his Genevieve young S. T. C.
8. With thousands else that, wandering up and down,
9. Quaint, privileged, liked and reputed well,
10. Made the great School a part of London Town
11. Patent as Paul's and vital as Bow Bell:
12. The old School nearing exile, day by day,
13. To certain clay-lands somewhere Horsham way.
