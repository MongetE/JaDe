1. Praise the generous gods for giving
2. In a world of wrath and strife,
3. With a little time for living,
4. Unto all the joy of life.

5. At whatever source we drink it,
6. Art or life or faith or wine,
7. In whatever terms we think it,
8. It is common and divine.

9. Praise the high gods, for in giving
10. This for man, and this alone,
11. They have made his chance for living
12. Shine the equal of their own.
