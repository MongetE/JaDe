1. Fresh from his fastnesses
2. Wholesome and spacious,
3. The North Wind, the mad huntsman,
4. Halloas on his white hounds
5. Over the grey, roaring
6. Reaches and ridges,
7. The forest of ocean,
8. The chace of the world.
9. Hark to the peal
10. Of the pack in full cry,
11. As he thongs them before him,
12. Swarming voluminous,
13. Weltering, wide-wallowing,
14. Till in a ruining
15. Chaos of energy,
16. Hurled on their quarry,
17. They crash into foam!

18. Old Indefatigable,
19. Time's right-hand man, the sea
20. Laughs as in joy
21. From his millions of wrinkles:
22. Laughs that his destiny,
23. Great with the greatness
24. Of triumphing order,
25. Shows as a dwarf
26. By the strength of his heart
27. And the might of his hands.

28. Master of masters,
29. O maker of heroes,
30. Thunder the brave,
31. Irresistible message:-
32. 'Life is worth Living
33. Through every grain of it,
34. From the foundations
35. To the last edge
36. Of the cornerstone, death.'
