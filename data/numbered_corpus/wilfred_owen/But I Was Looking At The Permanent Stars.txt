1. Bugles sang, saddening the evening air,
2. And bugles answered, sorrowful to hear.

3. Voices of boys were by the river-side.
4. Sleep mothered them; and left the twilight sad.
5. The shadow of the morrow weighed on men.

6. Voices of old despondency resigned,
7. Bowed by the shadow of the morrow, slept.
