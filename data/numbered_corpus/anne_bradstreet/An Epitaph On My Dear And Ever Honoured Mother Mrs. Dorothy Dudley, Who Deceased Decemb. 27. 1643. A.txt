1. A worthy Matron of unspotted life,
2. A loving Mother and obedient wife,
3. A friendly Neighbor, pitiful to poor,
4. Whom oft she fed, and clothed with her store;
5. To Servants wisely aweful, but yet kind,
6. And as they did, so they reward did find:
7. A true Instructer of her Family,
8. The which she ordered with dexterity.
9. The publick meetings ever did frequent,
10. And in her Closet constant hours she spent;
11. Religious in all her words and wayes,
12. Preparing still for death, till end of dayes:
13. Of all her Children, Children, liv'd to see,
14. Then dying, left a blessed memory.
