2. The rose new-opening saith,
3. And the dew of the morning saith,
4. (Fallen leaves and vanished dew)
5. Remember death.
6. Ding dong bell
7. Ding dong bell

8. (2)

9. May-moon thin and young
10. In the sky,
11. Ere you wax and wane
12. I shall die;
13. So my faltering breath,
14. So my tired heart saith,
15. That foretell me death.
16. Ding-dong
17. Ding-dong
18. Ding-dong ding-dong bell

19. (3)

20. 'Thy gold hair likes me well
21. And thy blue eyes,' he saith,
22. Who chooses where he will
23. And none may hinder - Death.
24. At head and feet for candles
25. Roses burning red,
26. The valley lilies tolling
27. For the early dead:
28. Ding-dong ding-dong
29. Ding-dong ding-dong
30. Ding-dong ding-dong bell
31. Ding-dong bell
