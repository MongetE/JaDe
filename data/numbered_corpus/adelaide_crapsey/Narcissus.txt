1. 'Boy, lying
2. Where the long grass
3. Edges the pool's brim,
4. What do you watch
5. There in the water? The blue
6. Colour of Heaven
7. Mirrored, repeated? the brown
8. Tree-trunks and branches
9. Waveringly imaged? These,
10. Boy, do you watch?'
11. 'Nay but mine eyes;
12. Nay but the trouble
13. Deep in mine eyes'
