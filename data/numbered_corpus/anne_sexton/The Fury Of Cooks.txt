1. Herbs, garlic,
2. cheese, please let me in!
3. Souffles, salad,
4. Parker House rolls,
5. please let me in!
6. Cook Helen,
7. why are you so cross,
8. why is your kitchen verboten?
9. Couldn't you just teach me
10. to bake a potato,
11. to bake a potato,
12. that charm,
13. that young prince?
14. No! No!
15. This is my county!
16. You shout silently.
17. Couldn't you just show me
18. the gravy. How you drill it out
19. of the stomach of that bird?
20. Helen, Helen,
21. let me in,
22. let me feel the flour,
23. is it blinding and frightening,
24. this stuff that makes cakes?
25. Helen, Helen,
26. the kitchen is your dog
27. and you pat it
28. and love it
29. and keep it clean.
30. But all these things,
31. all these dishes of things
32. come through the swinging door
33. and I don't know from where?
34. Give me some tomato aspic, Helen!
35. I don't want to be alone.
