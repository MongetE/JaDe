1. A red-hot needle
2. hangs out of him, he steers by it
3. as if it were a rudder, he
4. would get in the house any way he could
5. and then he would bounce from window
6. to ceiling, buzzing and looking for you.
7. Do not sleep for he is there wrapped in the curtain.
8. Do not sleep for he is there under the shelf.
9. Do not sleep for he wants to sew up your skin,
10. he want to leap into your body like a hammer
11. with a nail, do not sleep he wants to get into
12. your nose and make a transplant, he wants do not
13. sleep he wants to bury your fur and make
14. a nest of knives, he wants to slide under your
15. fingernail and push in a splinter, do not sleep
16. he wants to climb out of the toilet when you sit on it
17. and make a home in the embarrassed hair do not sleep
18. he wants you to walk into him as into a dark fire.
