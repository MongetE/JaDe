1. Everyone in me is a bird.
2. I am beating all my wings.
3. They wanted to cut you out
4. but they will not.
5. They said you were immeasurably empty
6. but you are not.
7. They said you were sick unto dying
8. but they were wrong.
9. You are singing like a school girl.
10. You are not torn.


11. Sweet weight,
12. in celebration of the woman I am
13. and of the soul of the woman I am
14. and of the central creature and its delight
15. I sing for you. I dare to live.
16. Hello, spirit. Hello, cup.
17. Fasten, cover. Cover that does contain.
18. Hello to the soil of the fields.
19. Welcome, roots.


20. Each cell has a life.
21. There is enough here to please a nation.
22. It is enough that the populace own these goods.
23. Any person, any commonwealth would say of it,
24. “It is good this year that we may plant again
25. and think forward to a harvest.
26. A blight had been forecast and has been cast out.”
27. Many women are singing together of this:
28. one is in a shoe factory cursing the machine,
29. one is at the aquarium tending a seal,
30. one is dull at the wheel of her Ford,
31. one is at the toll gate collecting,
32. one is tying the cord of a calf in Arizona,
33. one is straddling a cello in Russia,
34. one is shifting pots on the stove in Egypt,
35. one is painting her bedroom walls moon color,
36. one is dying but remembering a breakfast,
37. one is stretching on her mat in Thailand,
38. one is wiping the ass of her child,
39. one is staring out the window of a train
40. in the middle of Wyoming and one is
41. anywhere and some are everywhere and all
42. seem to be singing, although some can not
43. sing a note.


44. Sweet weight,
45. in celebration of the woman I am
46. let me carry a ten-foot scarf,
47. let me drum for the nineteen-year-olds,
48. let me carry bowls for the offering
49. (if that is my part).
50. Let me study the cardiovascular tissue,
51. let me examine the angular distance of meteors,
52. let me suck on the stems of flowers
53. (if that is my part).
54. Let me make certain tribal figures
55. (if that is my part).
56. For this thing the body needs
57. let me sing
58. for the supper,
59. for the kissing,
60. for the correct
61. yes.
