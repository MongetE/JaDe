1. While I, that reed-throated whisperer
2. Who comes at need, although not now as once
3. A clear articulation in the air,
4. But inwardly, surmise companions
5. Beyond the fling of the dull ass's hoof
6. - Ben Johnson's phrase - and find when June is come
7. At Kyle-na-no under that ancient roof
8. A sterner conscience and a friendlier home,
9. I can forgive even that wrong of wrongs,
10. Those undreamt accidents that have made me
11. - Seeing that Fame has perished that long while,
12. Being but a part of ancient ceremony -
13. Notorious, till all my priceless things
14. Are but a post the passing dogs defile.
