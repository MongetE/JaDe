1. I RANTED to the knave and fool,
2. But outgrew that school,
3. Would transform the part,
4. Fit audience found, but cannot rule
5. My fanatic heart.
6. I sought my betters:  though in each
7. Fine manners, liberal speech,
8. Turn hatred into sport,
9. Nothing said or done can reach
10. My fanatic heart,
11. Out of Ireland have we come.
12. Great hatred, little room,
13. Maimed us at the start.
14. I carry from my mother's womb
15. A fanatic heart.
