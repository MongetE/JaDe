1. AH, that Time could touch a form
2. That could show what Homer's age
3. Bred to be a hero's wage.
4. 'Were not all her life but storm
5. Would not painters paint a form
6. Of such noble lines,' I said,
7. 'Such a delicate high head,
8. All that sternness amid charm,
9. All that sweetness amid strength?'
10. Ah, but peace that comes at length,
11. Came when Time had touched her form.
