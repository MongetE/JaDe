1. I ASKED if I should pray.
2. But the Brahmin said,
3. 'pray for nothing, say
4. Every night in bed,
5. 'I have been a king,
6. I have been a slave,
7. Nor is there anything.
8. Fool, rascal, knave,
9. That I have not been,
10. And yet upon my breast
11. A myriad heads have lain.'''
12. That he might Set at rest
13. A boy's turbulent days
14. Mohini Chatterjee
15. Spoke these, or words like these,
16. I add in commentary,
17. 'Old lovers yet may have
18. All that time denied --
19. Grave is heaped on grave
20. That they be satisfied --
21. Over the blackened earth
22. The old troops parade,
23. Birth is heaped on Birth
24. That such cannonade
25. May thunder time away,
26. Birth-hour and death-hour meet,
27. Or, as great sages say,
28. Men dance on deathless feet.'
