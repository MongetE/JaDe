1. ACQUAINTANCE; companion;
2. One dear brilliant woman;
3. The best-endowed, the elect,
4. All by their youth undone,
5. All, all, by that inhuman
6. Bitter glory wrecked.
7. But I have straightened out
8. Ruin, wreck and wrack;
9. I toiled long years and at length
10. Came to so deep a thought
11. I can summon back
12. All their wholesome strength.
13. What images are these
14. That turn dull-eyed away,
15. Or Shift Time's filthy load,
16. Straighten aged knees,
17. Hesitate or stay?
18. What heads shake or nod?
