1. BALD heads forgetful of their sins,
2. Old, learned, respectable bald heads
3. Edit and annotate the lines
4. That young men, tossing on their beds,
5. Rhymed out in love's despair
6. To flatter beauty's ignorant ear.
7. All shuffle there; all cough in ink;
8. All wear the carpet with their shoes;
9. All think what other people think;
10. All know the man their neighbour knows.
11. Lord, what would they say
12. Did their Catullus walk that way?
