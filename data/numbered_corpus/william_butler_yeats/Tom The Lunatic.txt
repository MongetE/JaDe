1. Sang old Tom the lunatic
2. That sleeps under the canopy:
3. 'What change has put my thoughts astray
4. And eyes that had so keen a sight?
5. What has turned to smoking wick
6. Nature's pure unchanging light?

7. 'Huddon and Duddon and Daniel O'Leary.
8. Holy Joe, the beggar-man,
9. Wenching, drinking, still remain
10. Or sing a penance on the road;
11. Something made these eyeballs weary
12. That blinked and saw them in a shroud.

13. 'Whatever stands in field or flood,
14. Bird, beast, fish or man,
15. Mare or stallion, cock or hen,
16. Stands in God's unchanging eye
17. In all the vigour of its blood;
18. In that faith I live or die.'
