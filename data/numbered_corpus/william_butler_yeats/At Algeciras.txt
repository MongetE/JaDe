1. The heron-billed pale cattle-birds
2. That feed on some foul parasite
3. Of the Moroccan flocks and herds
4. Cross the narrow Straits to light
5. In the rich midnight of the garden trees
6. Till the dawn break upon those mingled seas.

7. Often at evening when a boy
8. Would I carry to a friend -
9. Hoping more substantial joy
10. Did an older mind commend -
11. Not such as are in Newton's metaphor,
12. But actual shells of Rosses' level shore.

13. Greater glory in the Sun,
14. An evening chill upon the air,
15. Bid imagination run
16. Much on the Great Questioner;
17. What He can question, what if questioned I
18. Can with a fitting confidence reply.
