1. III
2. Slim adolescence that a nymph has stripped,
3. Peleus on Thetis stares.
4. Her limbs are delicate as an eyelid,
5. Love has blinded him with tears;
6. But Thetis' belly listens.
7. Down the mountain walls
8. From where pan's cavern is
9. Intolerable music falls.
10. Foul goat-head, brutal arm appear,
11. Belly, shoulder, bum,
12. Flash fishlike; nymphs and satyrs
13. Copulate in the foam.
