1. BEAUTIFUL lofty things:  O'Leary's noble head;
2. My father upon the Abbey stage, before him a raging crowd:
3. 'This Land of Saints,' and then as the applause died out,
4. 'Of plaster Saints'; his beautiful mischievous head thrown back.
5. Standish O'Grady supporting himself between the tables
6. Speaking to a drunken audience high nonsensical words;
7. Augusta Gregory seated at her great ormolu table,
8. Her eightieth winter approaching:  'Yesterday he threatened my life.
9. I told him that nightly from six to seven I sat at this table,
10. The blinds drawn up'; Maud Gonne at Howth station waiting a train,
11. Pallas Athene in that straight back and arrogant head:
12. All the Olympians; a thing never known again.
