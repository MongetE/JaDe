1. WHAT if I bade you leave
2. The cavern of the mind?
3. There's better exercise
4. In the sunlight and wind.
5. I never bade you go
6. To Moscow or to Rome.
7. Renounce that drudgery,
8. Call the Muses home.
9. Seek those images
10. That constitute the wild,
11. The lion and the virgin,
12. The harlot and the child
13. Find in middle air
14. An eagle on the wing,
15. Recognise the five
16. That make the Muses sing.
