1. O BUT we talked at large before
2. The sixteen men were shot,
3. But who can talk of give and take,
4. What should be and what not
5. While those dead men are loitering there
6. To stir the boiling pot?
7. You say that we should still the land
8. Till Germany's overcome;
9. But who is there to argue that
10. Now Pearse is deaf and dumb?
11. And is their logic to outweigh
12. MacDonagh's bony thumb?
13. how could you dream they'd listen
14. That have an ear alone
15. For those new comrades they have found,
16. Lord Edward and Wolfe Tone,
17. Or meddle with our give and take
18. That converse bone to bone?
