1. 1. They named the child Orc, he grew
2. Fed with milk of Enitharmon

3. 2. Los awoke her; O sorrow & pain!
4. A tight'ning girdle grew,
5. Around his bosom. In sobbings
6. He burst the girdle in twain,
7. But still another girdle
8. Opressd his bosom, In sobbings
9. Again he burst it. Again
10. Another girdle succeeds
11. The girdle was form'd by day;
12. By night was burst in twain.

13. 3. These falling down on the rock
14. Into an iron Chain
15. In each other link by link lock'd

16. 4. They took Orc to the top of a mountain.
17. O how Enitharmon wept!
18. They chain'd his young limbs to the rock
19. With the Chain of Jealousy
20. Beneath Urizens deathful shadow

21. 5. The dead heard the voice of the child
22. And began to awake from sleep
23. All things. heard the voice of the child
24. And began to awake to life.

25. 6. And Urizen craving with hunger
26. Stung with the odours of Nature
27. Explor'd his dens around

28. 7. He form'd a line & a plummet
29. To divide the Abyss beneath.
30. He form'd a dividing rule:

31. 8. He formed scales to weigh;
32. He formed massy weights;
33. He formed a brazen quadrant;
34. He formed golden compasses
35. And began to explore the Abyss
36. And he planted a garden of fruits

37. 9. But Los encircled Enitharmon
38. With fires of Prophecy
39. From the sight of Urizen & Orc.

40. 10. And she bore an enormous race
