1. Eased eyes open, showed leaves.

2. Eyes laughing and childish
3. Ran among flowers of leaves 
4. And looked at light's bridge
5. Which led from leaf, upward, and back down to leaf.

6. Eyes uncertain
7. Tested each semblance
8. Light seemed to smile.

9. Eyes ran to the limit
10. To the last leaf
11. To the last vein of the least flower-leaf.

12. Light smiled,
13. And smiled and smiled

14. Eyes 
15. Darkened
16. Afraid suddenly
