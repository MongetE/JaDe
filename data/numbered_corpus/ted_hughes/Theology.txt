1. No, the serpent did not
2. Seduce Eve to the apple.
3. All that's simply
4. Corruption of the facts.

5. Adam ate the apple.
6. Eve ate Adam.
7. The serpent ate Eve.
8. This is the dark intestine.

9. The serpent, meanwhile,
10. Sleeps his meal off in Paradise -
11. Smiling to hear
12. God's querulous calling.
