1. Teach me I am forgotten by the dead
2. And that the dead is by herself forgot
3. And I no longer would keep terms with me.
4. I would not murder, steal, or fornicate,
5. Nor with ambition break the peace of towns
6. But I would bury my ambition
7. The hope & action of my sovereign soul
8. In miserable ruin. Nor a hope
9. Should ever make a holiday for me
10. I would not be the fool of accident
11. I would not have a project seek an end
12. That needed aught
13. Beyond the handful of my present means
14. The sun of Duty drop from his firmament
15. To be a rushlight for each petty end
16. I would not harm my fellow men
17. On this low argument, 'twould harm myself.
