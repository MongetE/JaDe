1. HENCEFORTH, please God, forever I forego
2. The yoke of men's opinions. I will be
3. Light-hearted as a bird, and live with God.
4. I find him in the bottom of my heart,
5. I hear continually his voice therein.

7. The little needle always knows the North,
8. The little bird remembereth his note,
9. And this wise Seer within me never errs.
10. I never taught it what it teaches me;
11. I only follow, when I act aright.
