1. "May be true what I had heard,
2. Earth's a howling wilderness
3. Truculent with fraud and force,"
4. Said I, strolling through the pastures,
5. And along the riverside.
6. Caught among the blackberry vines,
7. Feeding on the Ethiops sweet,
8. Pleasant fancies overtook me:
9. I said, "What influence me preferred
10. Elect to dreams thus beautiful?"
11. The vines replied, "And didst thou deem
12. No wisdom to our berries went?"
