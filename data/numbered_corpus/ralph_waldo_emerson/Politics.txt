1. Gold and iron are good
2. To buy iron and gold;
3. All earth's fleece and food
4. For their like are sold.
5. Hinted Merlin wise,
6. Proved Napoleon great,
7. Nor kind nor coinage buys
8. Aught above its rate.
9. Fear, Craft, and Avarice
10. Cannot rear a State.
11. Out of dust to build
12. What is more than dust,--
13. Walls Amphion piled
14. Phoebus stablish must.
15. When the Muses nine
16. When the Virtues meet,
17. Find to their design
18. An Atlantic seat,
19. By green orchard boughs
20. Fended from the heat,
21. Where the statesman ploughs
22. Furrow for the wheat,--
23. When the Church is social worth,
24. When the state-house is the hearth,
25. Then the perfect State is come,
26. The republican at home.
