1. Jaya Surya

2. GOLDEN sun of victory, born
3. In my life's unclouded morn,
4. In my lambent sky of love,
5. May your growing glory prove
6. Sacred to your consecration,
7. To my heart and to my nation.
8. Sun of victory, may you be
9. Sun of song and liberty.

10. Padmaja

11. Lotus-maiden, you who claim
12. All the sweetness of your name,
13. Lakshmi, fortune's queen, defend you,
14. Lotus-born like you, and send you
15. Balmy moons of love to bless you,
16. Gentle joy-winds to caress you.
17. Lotus-maiden, may you be
18. Fragrant of all ecstasy.

19. Ranadheera

20. Little lord of battle, hail
21. In your newly-tempered mail!
22. Learn to conquer, learn to fight
23. In the foremost flanks of right,
24. Like Valmiki's heroes bold,
25. Rubies girt in epic gold.
26. Lord of battle, may you be,
27. Lord of love and chivalry.

28. Lilamani

29. Limpid jewel of delight
30. Severed from the tender night
31. Of your sheltering mother-mine,
32. Leap and sparkle, dance and shine,
33. Blithely and securely set
34. In love's magic coronet.
35. Living jewel, may you be
36. Laughter-bound and sorrow-free.
