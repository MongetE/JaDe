1. TREAD lightly, she is near
2. Under the snow,
3. Speak gently, she can hear
4. The daisies grow.

5. All her bright golden hair
6. Tarnished with rust,
7. She that was young and fair
8. Fallen to dust.

9. Lily-like, white as snow,
10. She hardly knew
11. She was a woman, so
12. Sweetly she grew.

13. Coffin-board, heavy stone,
14. Lie on her breast,
15. I vex my heart alone
16. She is at rest.

17. Peace, Peace, she cannot hear
18. Lyre or sonnet,
19. All my life's buried here,
20. Heap earth upon it.

21. AVIGNON.
