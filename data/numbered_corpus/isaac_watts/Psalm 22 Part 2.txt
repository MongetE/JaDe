1. v.20,21,27-
2. C. M.
3. Christ's sufferings and kingdom.

4. "Now from the roaring lion's rage,
5. O Lord, protect thy Son,
6. Nor leave thy darling to engage
7. The powers of hell alone."

8. Thus did our suff'ring Savior pray,
9. With mighty cries and tears;
10. God heard him in that dreadful day,
11. And chased away his fears.

12. Great was the vict'ry of his death,
13. His throne exalted high;
14. And all the kindreds of the earth
15. Shall worship or shall die.

16. A num'rous offspring must arise
17. From his expiring groans;
18. They shall be reckoned in his eyes
19. For daughters and for sons.

20. The meek and humble souls shall see
21. His table richly spread;
22. And all that seek the Lord shall be
23. With joys immortal fed.

24. The isles shall know the righteousness
25. Of our incarnate God,
26. And nations yet unborn profess
27. Salvation in his blood.
