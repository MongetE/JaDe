1. Meditation of heaven; or, The joy of faith.

2. My thoughts surmount these lower skies,
3. And look within the veil;
4. There springs of endless pleasure rise,
5. The waters never fail.

6. There I behold, with sweet delight,
7. The blessed Three in One;
8. And strong affections fix my sight
9. On God's incarnate Son.

10. His promise stands for ever firm,
11. His grace shall ne'er depart;
12. He binds my name upon his arm,
13. And seals it on his heart.

14. Light are the pains that nature brings;
15. How short our sorrows are,
16. When with eternal future things
17. The present we compare!

18. I would not be a stranger still
19. To that celestial place,
20. Where I for ever hope to dwell
21. Near my Redeemer's face.
