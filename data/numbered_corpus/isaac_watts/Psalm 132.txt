1. v.5,13-
2. L. M.
3. At the settlement of a church, or the ordination of a minister.

4. Where shall we go to seek and find
5. An habitation for our God,
6. A dwelling for th' Eternal Mind,
7. Among the sons of flesh and blood?

8. The God of Jacob chose the hill
9. Of Zion for his ancient rest;
10. And Zion is his dwelling still,
11. His church is with his presence blessed.

12. Here will I fix my gracious throne,
13. And reign for ever, saith the Lord;
14. Here shall my power and love be known,
15. And blessings shall attend my word.

16. Here will I meet the hungry poor,
17. And fill their souls with living bread;
18. Sinners that wait before my door
19. With sweet provision shall be fed.

20. Girded with truth, and clothed with grace,
21. My priests, my ministers, shall shine
22. Not Aaron in his costly dress
23. Made an appearance so divine.

24. The saints, unable to contain
25. Their inward joys, shall shout and sing;
26. The Son of David here shall reign,
27. And Zion triumph in her King.

28. [Jesus shall see a num'rous seed
29. Born here t' uphold his glorious name;
30. His crown shall flourish on his head,
31. While all his foes are clothed with shame.]
