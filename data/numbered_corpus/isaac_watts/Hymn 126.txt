1. Charity and uncharitableness.

2. Rom. 14:17,19; 1 Cor. 10:32.

3. Not diff'rent food, or diff'rent dress,
4. Compose the kingdom of our Lord;
5. But peace, and joy, and righteousness,
6. Faith, and obedience to his word.

7. When weaker Christians we despise,
8. We do the gospel mighty wrong;
9. For God, the gracious and the wise,
10. Receives the feeble with the strong.

11. Let pride and wrath be banished hence;
12. Meekness and love our souls pursue;
13. Nor shall our practice give offence
14. To saints, the Gentile or the Jew.
