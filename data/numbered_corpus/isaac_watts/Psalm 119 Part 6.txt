1. Holiness and comfort from the word.

2. ver.

3. Lord, I esteem thy judgments right,
4. And all thy statutes just;
5. Thence I maintain a constant fight
6. With every flatt'ring lust.

7. ver. 97,9

8. Thy precepts often I survey;
9. I keep thy law in sight,
10. Through all the business of the day,
11. To form my actions right.

12. ver.

13. My heart in midnight silence cries,
14. "How sweet thy comforts be!"
15. My thoughts in holy wonder rise,
16. And bring their thanks to thee.

17. ver.

18. And when my spirit drinks her fill
19. At some good word of thine,
20. Not mighty men that share the spoil
21. Have joys compared to mine.
