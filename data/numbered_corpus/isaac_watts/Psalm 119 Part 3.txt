1. Professions of sincerity, repentance, and obedience.

2. ver. 57,

3. Thou art my portion, O my God;
4. Soon as I know thy way,
5. My heart makes haste t' obey thy word,
6. And suffers no delay.

7. ver. 30,

8. I choose the path of heav'nly truth,
9. And glory in my choice;
10. Not all the riches of the earth
11. Could make me so rejoice.

12. The testimonies of thy grace
13. I set before my eyes;
14. Thence I derive my daily strength,
15. And there my comfort lies.

16. ver.

17. If once I wander from thy path,
18. I think upon my ways,
19. Then turn my feet to thy commands,
20. And trust thy pard'ning grace.

21. ver. 94,

22. Now I am thine, for ever thine,
23. O save thy servant, Lord;
24. Thou art my shield, my hiding-place;
25. My hope is in thy word.

26. ver.

27. Thou hast inclined this heart of mine
28. Thy statutes to fulfil;
29. And thus, till mortal life shall end,
30. Would I perform thy will.
