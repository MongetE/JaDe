1. Self-examination; or, Evidences of grace.

2. Judge me, O Lord, and prove my ways,
3. And try my reins, and try my heart
4. My faith upon thy promise stays,
5. Nor from thy law my feet depart.

6. I hate to walk, I hate to sit,
7. With men of vanity and lies
8. The scoffer and the hypocrite
9. Are the abhorrence of mine eyes.

10. Amongst thy saints will I appear
11. With frauds well washed in innocence;
12. But when I stand before thy bar,
13. The blood of Christ is my defence.

14. I love thy habitation, Lord,
15. The temple where thine honors dwell;
16. There shall I hear thine holy word,
17. And there thy works of wonder tell.

18. Let not my soul be joined at last
19. With men of treachery and blood,
20. Since I my days on earth have passed
21. Among the saints, and near my God.
