1. v.12-
2. L. M.
3. Grace above riches; or, The happy nation.

4. Happy the city where their sons,
5. Like pillars round a palace set,
6. And daughters, bright as polished stones,
7. Give strength and beauty to the state.

8. Happy the country where the sheep,
9. Cattle, and corn, have large increase;
10. Where men securely work or sleep,
11. Nor sons of plunder break the peace.

12. Happy the nation thus endowed,
13. But more divinely blest are those
14. On whom the all-sufficient God
15. Himself with all his grace bestows.
