1. v.1-7,11-
2. C. M.
3. The greatness of God.

4. Long as I live I'll bless thy name,
5. My King, my God of love;
6. My work and joy shall be the same
7. In the bright world above.

8. Great is the Lord, his power unknown,
9. And let his praise be great;
10. I'll sing the honors of thy throne,
11. Thy works of grace repeat.

12. Thy grace shall dwell upon my tongue;
13. And while my lips rejoice,
14. The men that hear my sacred song
15. Shall join their cheerful voice.

16. Fathers to sons shall teach thy name,
17. And children learn thy ways;
18. Ages to come thy truth proclaim,
19. And nations sound thy praise.

20. Thy glorious deeds of ancient date
21. Shall through the world be known;
22. Thine arm of power, thy heav'nly state,
23. With public splendor shown.

24. The world is managed by thy hands,
25. Thy saints are ruled by love;
26. And thine eternal kingdom stands,
27. Though rocks and hills remove.
