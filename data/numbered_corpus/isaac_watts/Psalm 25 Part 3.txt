1. v.15-
2. S. M.
3. Distress of soul; or, Backsliding and desertion.

4. Mine eyes and my desire
5. Are ever to the Lord;
6. I love to plead his promises,
7. And rest upon his word.

8. Turn, turn thee to my soul,
9. Bring thy salvation near;
10. When will thy hand release my feet
11. Out of the deadly snare?

12. When shall the sovereign grace
13. Of my forgiving God
14. Restore me from those dangerous ways
15. My wand'ring feet have trod?

16. The tumult of my thoughts
17. Doth but enlarge my woe;
18. My spirit languishes, my heart
19. Is desolate and low.

20. With ev'ry morning light
21. My sorrow new begins;
22. Look on my anguish and my pain,
23. And pardon all my sins.

24. PAUSE.

25. Behold the hosts of hell,
26. How cruel is their hate!
27. Against my life they rise, and join
28. Their fury with deceit.

29. O keep my soul from death,
30. Nor put my hope to shame,
31. For I have placed my only trust
32. In my Redeemer's name.

33. With humble faith I wait
34. To see thy face again:
35. Of Isr'el it shall ne'er be said,
36. He sought the Lord in vain.
