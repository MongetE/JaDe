1. The witch that came (the withered hag)
2. To wash the steps with pail and rag
3. Was once the beauty Abishag,

4. The picture pride of Hollywood.
5. Too many fall from great and good
6. For you to doubt the likelihood.

7. Die early and avoid the fate.
8. Or if predestined to die late,
9. Make up your mind to die in state.

10. Make the whole stock exchange your own!
11. If need be occupy a throne,
12. Where nobody can call you crone.

13. Some have relied on what they knew,
14. Others on being simply true.
15. What worked for them might work for you.

16. No memory of having starred
17. Atones for later disregard
18. Or keeps the end from being hard.

19. Better to go down dignified
20. With boughten friendship at your side
21. Than none at all. Provide, provide!
