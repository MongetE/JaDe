1. Seek not in me the big I capital,
2. Not yet the little dotted in me seek.
3. If I have in me any I at all,
4. 'Tis the iota subscript of the Greek.
5. So small am I as an attention beggar.
6. The letter you will find me subscript to
7. Is neither alpha, eta, nor omega,
8. But upsilon which is the Greek for you.
