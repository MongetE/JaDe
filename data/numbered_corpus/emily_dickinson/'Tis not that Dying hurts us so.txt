1. 'Tis not that Dying hurts us so —
2. 'Tis Living — hurts us more —
3. But Dying — is a different way —
4. A Kind behind the Door —

5. The Southern Custom — of the Bird —
6. That ere the Frosts are due —
7. Accepts a better Latitude —
8. We — are the Birds — that stay.

9. The Shrivers round Farmers' doors —
10. For whose reluctant Crumb —
11. We stipulate — till pitying Snows
12. Persuade our Feathers Home.
