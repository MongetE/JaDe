

1. They called me to the Window, for
2. " 'Twas Sunset"—Some one said—
3. I only saw a Sapphire Farm—
4. And just a Single Herd—

5. Of Opal Cattle—feeding far
6. Upon so vain a Hill—
7. As even while I looked—dissolved—
8. Nor Cattle were—nor Soil—

9. But in their stead—a Sea—displayed—
10. And Ships—of such a size
11. As Crew of Mountains—could afford—
12. And Decks—to seat the skies—

13. This—too—the Showman rubbed away—
14. And when I looked again—
15. Nor Farm—nor Opal Herd—was there—
16. Nor Mediterranean—
