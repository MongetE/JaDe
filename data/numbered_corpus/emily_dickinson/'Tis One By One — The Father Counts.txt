

1. 'Tis One by One — the Father counts —
2. And then a Tract between
3. Set Cypherless — to teach the Eye
4. The Value of its Ten —

5. Until the peevish Student
6. Acquire the Quick of Skill —
7. Then Numerals are dowered back —
8. Adorning all the Rule —

9. 'Tis mostly Slate and Pencil —
10. And Darkness on the School
11. Distracts the Children's fingers —
12. Still the Eternal Rule

13. Regards least Cypherer alike
14. With Leader of the Band —
15. And every separate Urchin's Sum —
16. Is fashioned for his hand —
