1. Tell as a Marksman -  were forgotten
2. Tell -  this Day endures
3. Ruddy as that coeval Apple
4. The Tradition bears -

5. Fresh as Mankind that humble story
6. Though a statelier Tale
7. Grown in the Repetition hoary
8. Scarcely would prevail -

9. Tell had a son -  The ones that knew it
10. Need not linger here -
11. Those who did not to Human Nature
12. Will subscribe a Tear -

13. Tell would not bare his Head
14. In Presence
15. Of the Ducal Hat -
16. Threatened for that with Death -  by Gessler -
17. Tyranny bethought

18. Make of his only Boy a Target
19. That surpasses Death -
20. Stolid to Love's supreme entreaty
21. Not forsook of Faith -

22. Mercy of the Almighty begging -
23. Tell his Arrow sent -
24. God it is said replies in Person
25. When the cry is meant -
