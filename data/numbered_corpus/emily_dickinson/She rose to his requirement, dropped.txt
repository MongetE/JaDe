1. She rose to his requirement, dropped
2. The playthings of her life
3. To take the honorable work
4. Of woman and of wife.

5. If aught she missed in her new day
6. Of amplitude, or awe,
7. Or first prospective, or the gold
8. In using wore away,

9. It lay unmentioned, as the sea
10. Develops pearl and weed,
11. But only to himself is known
12. The fathoms they abide.
