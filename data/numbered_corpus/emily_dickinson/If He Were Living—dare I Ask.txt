

1. If He were living—dare I ask—
2. And how if He be dead—
3. And so around the Words I went—
4. Of meeting them—afraid—

5. I hinted Changes—Lapse of Time—
6. The Surfaces of Years—
7. I touched with Caution—lest they crack—
8. And show me to my fears—

9. Reverted to adjoining Lives—
10. Adroitly turning out
11. Wherever I suspected Graves—
12. 'Twas prudenter—I thought—

13. And He—I pushed—with sudden force—
14. In face of the Suspense—
15. "Was buried"—"Buried"! "He!"
16. My Life just holds the Trench—
