

1. One Day is there of the Series
2. Termed Thanksgiving Day.
3. Celebrated part at Table
4. Part in Memory.

5. Neither Patriarch nor Pussy
6. I dissect the Play
7. Seems it to my Hooded thinking
8. Reflex Holiday.

9. Had there been no sharp Subtraction
10. From the early Sum—
11. Not an Acre or a Caption
12. Where was once a Room—

13. Not a Mention, whose small Pebble
14. Wrinkled any Sea,
15. Unto Such, were such Assembly
16. 'Twere Thanksgiving Day.
