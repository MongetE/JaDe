

1. I showed her Heights she never saw—
2. "Would'st Climb," I said?
3. She said—"Not so"—
4. "With me—" I said—With me?
5. I showed her Secrets—Morning's Nest—
6. The Rope the Nights were put across—
7. And now—"Would'st have me for a Guest?"
8. She could not find her Yes—
9. And then, I brake my life—And Lo,
10. A Light, for her, did solemn glow,
11. The larger, as her face withdrew—
12. And could she, further, "No"?
