1. Not with a club, the Heart is broken,
2. Nor with a stone;
3. A whip, so small you could not see it,
4. I've known

5. To lash the magic creature
6. Till it fell,
7. Yet that whip's name too noble
8. Then to tell.

9. Magnanimous of bird
10. By boy descried,
11. To sing unto the stone
12. Of which it died.
