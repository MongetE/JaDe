

1. He strained my faith—
2. Did he find it supple?
3. Shook my strong trust—
4. Did it then—yield?

5. Hurled my belief—
6. But—did he shatter—it?
7. Racked—with suspense—
8. Not a nerve failed!

9. Wrung me—with Anguish—
10. But I never doubted him—
11. 'Tho' for what wrong
12. He did never say—

13. Stabbed—while I sued
14. His sweet forgiveness—
15. Jesus—it's your little "John"!
16. Don't you know—me?
