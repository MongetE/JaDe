

1. The Sunrise runs for Both—
2. The East—Her Purple Troth
3. Keeps with the Hill—
4. The Noon unwinds Her Blue
5. Till One Breadth cover Two—
6. Remotest—still—

7. Nor does the Night forget
8. A Lamp for Each—to set—
9. Wicks wide away—
10. The North—Her blazing Sign
11. Erects in Iodine—
12. Till Both—can see—

13. The Midnight's Dusky Arms
14. Clasp Hemispheres, and Homes
15. And so
16. Upon Her Bosom—One—
17. And One upon Her Hem—
18. Both lie—
