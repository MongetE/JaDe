

1. I make His Crescent fill or lack—
2. His Nature is at Full
3. Or Quarter—as I signify—
4. His Tides—do I control—

5. He holds superior in the Sky
6. Or gropes, at my Command
7. Behind inferior Clouds—or round
8. A Mist's slow Colonnade—

9. But since We hold a Mutual Disc—
10. And front a Mutual Day—
11. Which is the Despot, neither knows—
12. Nor Whose—the Tyranny—
