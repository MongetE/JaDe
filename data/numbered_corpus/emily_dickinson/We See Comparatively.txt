1. We see—Comparatively—
2. The Thing so towering high
3. We could not grasp its segment
4. Unaided—Yesterday—

5. This Morning's finer Verdict—
6. Makes scarcely worth the toil—
7. A furrow—Our Cordillera—
8. Our Apennine—a Knoll—

9. Perhaps 'tis kindly—done us—
10. The Anguish—and the loss—
11. The wrenching—for His Firmament
12. The Thing belonged to us—

13. To spare these Striding Spirits
14. Some Morning of Chagrin—
15. The waking in a Gnat's—embrace—
16. Our Giants—further on—
