

1. Only a Shrine, but Mine—
2. I made the Taper shine—
3. Madonna dim, to whom all Feet may come,
4. Regard a Nun—

5. Thou knowest every Woe—
6. Needless to tell thee—so—
7. But can'st thou do
8. The Grace next to it—heal?
9. That looks a harder skill to us—
10. Still—just as easy, if it be thy Will
11. To thee—Grant me—
12. Thou knowest, though, so Why tell thee?
