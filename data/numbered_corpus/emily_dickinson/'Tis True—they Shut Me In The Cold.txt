

1. 'Tis true—They shut me in the Cold—
2. But then—Themselves were warm
3. And could not know the feeling 'twas—
4. Forget it—Lord—of Them—

5. Let not my Witness hinder Them
6. In Heavenly esteem—
7. No Paradise could be—Conferred
8. Through Their beloved Blame—

9. The Harm They did—was short—And since
10. Myself—who bore it—do—
11. Forgive Them—Even as Myself—
12. Or else—forgive not me—
