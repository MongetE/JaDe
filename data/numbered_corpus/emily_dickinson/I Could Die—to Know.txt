

1. I could die—to know—
2. 'Tis a trifling knowledge—
3. News-Boys salute the Door—
4. Carts—joggle by—
5. Morning's bold face—stares in the window—
6. Were but mine—the Charter of the least Fly—

7. Houses hunch the House
8. With their Brick Shoulders—
9. Coals—from a Rolling Load—rattle—how—near—
10. To the very Square—His foot is passing—
11. Possibly, this moment—
12. While I—dream—Here—
