

1. Most she touched me by her muteness—
2. Most she won me by the way
3. She presented her small figure—
4. Plea itself—for Charity—

5. Were a Crumb my whole possession—
6. Were there famine in the land—
7. Were it my resource from starving—
8. Could I such a plea withstand—

9. Not upon her knee to thank me
10. Sank this Beggar from the Sky—
11. But the Crumb partook—departed—
12. And returned On High—

13. I supposed—when sudden
14. Such a Praise began
15. 'Twas as Space sat singing
16. To herself—and men—

17. 'Twas the Winged Beggar—
18. Afterward I learned
19. To her Benefactor
20. Making Gratitude
