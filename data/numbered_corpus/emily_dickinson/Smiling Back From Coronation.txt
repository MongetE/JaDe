

1. Smiling back from Coronation
2. May be Luxury—
3. On the Heads that started with us—
4. Being's Peasantry—

5. Recognizing in Procession
6. Ones We former knew—
7. When Ourselves were also dusty—
8. Centuries ago—

9. Had the Triumph no Conviction
10. Of how many be—
11. Stimulated—by the Contrast—
12. Unto Misery—
