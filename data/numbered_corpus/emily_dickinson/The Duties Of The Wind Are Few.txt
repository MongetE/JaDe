1. The duties of the Wind are few,
2. To cast the ships, at Sea,
3. Establish March, the Floods escort,
4. And usher Liberty.

5. The pleasures of the Wind are broad,
6. To dwell Extent among,
7. Remain, or wander,
8. Speculate, or Forests entertain.

9. The kinsmen of the Wind are Peaks
10. Azof -  the Equinox,
11. Also with Bird and Asteroid
12. A bowing intercourse.

13. The limitations of the Wind
14. Do he exist, or die,
15. Too wise he seems for Wakelessness,
16. However, know not i.
