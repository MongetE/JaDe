

1. Severer Service of myself
2. I—hastened to demand
3. To fill the awful Vacuum
4. Your life had left behind—

5. I worried Nature with my Wheels
6. When Hers had ceased to run—
7. When she had put away Her Work
8. My own had just begun.

9. I strove to weary Brain and Bone—
10. To harass to fatigue
11. The glittering Retinue of nerves—
12. Vitality to clog

13. To some dull comfort Those obtain
14. Who put a Head away
15. They knew the Hair to—
16. And forget the color of the Day—

17. Affliction would not be appeased—
18. The Darkness braced as firm
19. As all my stratagem had been
20. The Midnight to confirm—

21. No Drug for Consciousness—can be—
22. Alternative to die
23. Is Nature's only Pharmacy
24. For Being's Malady—
