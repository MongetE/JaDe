1. I reckon—when I count it all—
2. First—Poets—Then the Sun—
3. Then Summer—Then the Heaven of God—
4. And then—the List is done—

5. But, looking back—the First so seems
6. To Comprehend the Whole—
7. The Others look a needless Show—
8. So I write—Poets—All—

9. Their Summer—lasts a Solid Year—
10. They can afford a Sun
11. The East—would deem extravagant—
12. And if the Further Heaven—

13. Be Beautiful as they prepare
14. For Those who worship Them—
15. It is too difficult a Grace—
16. To justify the Dream—
