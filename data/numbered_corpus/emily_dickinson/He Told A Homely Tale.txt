

1. He told a homely tale
2. And spotted it with tears—
3. Upon his infant face was set
4. The Cicatrice of years—

5. All crumpled was the cheek
6. No other kiss had known
7. Than flake of snow, divided with
8. The Redbreast of the Barn—

9. If Mother—in the Grave—
10. Or Father—on the Sea—
11. Or Father in the Firmament—
12. Or Brethren, had he—

13. If Commonwealth below,
14. Or Commonwealth above
15. Have missed a Barefoot Citizen—
16. I've ransomed it—alive—
