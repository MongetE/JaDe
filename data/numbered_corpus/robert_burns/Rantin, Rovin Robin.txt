1. THERE 1 was a lad was born in Kyle,
2. But whatna day o' whatna style,
3. I doubt it's hardly worth the while
4. To be sae nice wi' Robin.


5. Chor.—Robin was a rovin' boy,
6. Rantin', rovin', rantin', rovin',
7. Robin was a rovin' boy,
8. Rantin', rovin', Robin!


9. Our monarch's hindmost year but ane
10. Was five-and-twenty days begun, 2
11. 'Twas then a blast o' Janwar' win'
12. Blew hansel in on Robin.
13. Robin was, &c.

14. The gossip keekit in his loof,
15. Quo' scho, "Wha lives will see the proof,
16. This waly boy will be nae coof:
17. I think we'll ca' him Robin."
18. Robin was, &c.

19. "He'll hae misfortunes great an' sma',
20. But aye a heart aboon them a',
21. He'll be a credit till us a'—
22. We'll a' be proud o' Robin."
23. Robin was, &c.

24. "But sure as three times three mak nine,
25. I see by ilka score and line,
26. This chap will dearly like our kin',
27. So leeze me on thee! Robin."
28. Robin was, &c.

29. "Guid faith," quo', scho, "I doubt you gar
30. The bonie lasses lie aspar;
31. But twenty fauts ye may hae waur
32. So blessins on thee! Robin."
33. Robin was, &c.
