1. SIR,Yours this moment I unseal,
2. And faith I'm gay and hearty!
3. To tell the truth and shame the deil,
4. I am as fou as Bartie:
5. But Foorsday, sir, my promise leal,
6. Expect me o' your partie,
7. If on a beastie I can speel,
8. Or hurl in a cartie.
9. YOURS,ROBERT BURNS.MAUCHLIN, Monday night, 10 o'clock.
