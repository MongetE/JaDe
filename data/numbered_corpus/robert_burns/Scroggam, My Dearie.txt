1. THERE was a wife wonn'd in Cockpen,
2. Scroggam;
3. She brew'd gude ale for gentlemen;
4. Sing auld Cowl lay ye down by me,
5. Scroggam, my dearie, ruffum.

6. The gudewife's dochter fell in a fever,
7. Scroggam;
8. The priest o' the parish he fell in anither;
9. Sing auld Cowl lay ye down by me,
10. Scroggam, my dearie, ruffum.

11. They laid the twa i' the bed thegither,
12. Scroggam;
13. That the heat o' the tane might cool the tither;
14. Sing auld Cowl, lay ye down by me,
15. Scroggam, my dearie, ruffum.
