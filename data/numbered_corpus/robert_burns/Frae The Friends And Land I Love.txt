1. FRAE the friends and land I love,
2. Driv'n by Fortune's felly spite;
3. Frae my best belov'd I rove,
4. Never mair to taste delight:
5. Never mair maun hope to find
6. Ease frae toil, relief frae care;
7. When Remembrance wracks the mind,
8. Pleasures but unveil despair.


9. Brightest climes shall mirk appear,
10. Desert ilka blooming shore,
11. Till the Fates, nae mair severe,
12. Friendship, love, and peace restore,
13. Till Revenge, wi' laurel'd head,
14. Bring our banished hame again;
15. And ilk loyal, bonie lad
16. Cross the seas, and win his ain.
