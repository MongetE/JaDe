1. O wilt thou go wi' me, sweet Tibbie Dunbar?
2. O wilt thou go wi' me, sweet Tibbie Dunbar?
3. Wilt thou ride on a horse, or be drawn in a car,
4. Or walk by my side, O sweet Tibbie Dunbar?

5. I care na thy daddie, his lands and his money,
6. I care na thy kin, sae high and sae lordly;
7. But sae that thou'lt hae me for better for waur,
8. And come in thy coatie, sweet Tibbie Dunbar.
