1. WILLIE WASTLE dwalt on Tweed,
2. The spot they ca'd it Linkumdoddie;
3. Willie was a wabster gude,
4. Could stown a clue wi' ony body:
5. He had a wife was dour and din,
6. O Tinkler Maidgie was her mither;
7. Sic a wife as Willie had,
8. I wad na gie a button for her!


9. She has an e'e, she has but ane,
10. The cat has twa the very colour;
11. Five rusty teeth, forbye a stump,
12. A clapper tongue wad deave a miller:
13. A whiskin beard about her mou',
14. Her nose and chin they threaten ither;
15. Sic a wife as Willie had,
16. I wadna gie a button for her!

17. She's bow-hough'd, she's hein-shin'd,
18. Ae limpin leg a hand-breed shorter;
19. She's twisted right, she's twisted left,
20. To balance fair in ilka quarter:
21. She has a lump upon her breast,
22. The twin o' that upon her shouther;
23. Sic a wife as Willie had,
24. I wadna gie a button for her!

25. Auld baudrons by the ingle sits,
26. An' wi' her loof her face a-washin;
27. But Willie's wife is nae sae trig,
28. She dights her grunzie wi' a hushion;
29. Her walie nieves like midden-creels,
30. Her face wad fyle the Logan Water;
31. Sic a wife as Willie had,
32. I wadna gie a button for her!
