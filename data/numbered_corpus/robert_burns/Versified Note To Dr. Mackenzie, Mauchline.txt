1. FRIDAY first's the day appointed
2. By the Right Worshipful anointed,
3. To hold our grand procession;
4. To get a blad o' Johnie's morals,
5. And taste a swatch o' Manson's barrels
6. I' the way of our profession.
7. The Master and the Brotherhood
8. Would a' be glad to see you;
9. For me I would be mair than proud
10. To share the mercies wi' you.
11. If Death, then, wi' skaith, then,
12. Some mortal heart is hechtin,
13. Inform him, and storm him,
14. That Saturday you'll fecht him.
