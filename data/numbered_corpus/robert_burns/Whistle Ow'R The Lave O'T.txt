1. My mither sent me tae the moss
2. For to gaither peats and dross.
3. I cowpit the cairt and hanged the horse
4. An whistle ow'r the lave o't.

5. My mither sent me tae the well
6. Better her had gaen hersel'.
7. I fell ower and broke my pail,
8. An' whistle ow'r the lave o't.

9. My mither sent me tae the sea
10. For tae gaither mussels three,
11. A sailor lad fell in wi' me
12. An' whistle ow'r the lave o't.

13. My mither sent me tae the moss
14. For tae gaither peats and dross.
15. I cowpit the cairt and hanged the horse,
16. And whistle ow'r the lave o't.
