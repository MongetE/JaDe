1. PEG NICHOLSON was a good bay mare,
2. As ever trod on airn;
3. But now she's floating down the Nith,
4. And past the mouth o' Cairn.


5. Peg Nicholson was a good bay mare,
6. An' rode thro' thick and thin;
7. But now she's floating down the Nith,
8. And wanting even the skin.


9. Peg Nicholson was a good bay mare,
10. And ance she bore a priest;
11. But now she's floating down the Nith,
12. For Solway fish a feast.


13. Peg Nicholson was a good bay mare,
14. An' the priest he rode her sair;
