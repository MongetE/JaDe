1. HEY, the dusty Miller,
2. And his dusty coat,
3. He will win a shilling,
4. Or he spend a groat:
5. Dusty was the coat,
6. Dusty was the colour,
7. Dusty was the kiss
8. That I gat frae the Miller.


9. Hey, the dusty Miller,
10. And his dusty sack;
11. Leeze me on the calling
12. Fills the dusty peck:
13. Fills the dusty peck,
14. Brings the dusty siller;
15. I wad gie my coatie
16. For the dusty Miller.
