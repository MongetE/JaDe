1. RIGHT, sir! your text I'll prove it true,
2. Tho' heretics may laugh;
3. For instance, there's yourself just now,
4. God knows, an unco calf.


5. And should some patron be so kind,
6. As bless you wi' a kirk,
7. I doubt na, sir but then we'll find,
8. Ye're still as great a stirk.


9. But, if the lover's raptur'd hour,
10. Shall ever be your lot,
11. Forbid it, ev'ry heavenly Power,
12. You e'er should be a stot!


13. Tho' when some kind connubial dear
14. Your but-and-ben adorns,
15. The like has been that you may wear
16. A noble head of horns.


17. And, in your lug, most reverend James,
18. To hear you roar and rowt,
19. Few men o' sense will doubt your claims
20. To rank amang the nowt.
