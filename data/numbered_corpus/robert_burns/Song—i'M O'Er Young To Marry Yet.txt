5. I AM my mammny's ae bairn,
6. Wi' unco folk I weary, sir;
7. And lying in a man's bed,
8. I'm fley'd it mak me eerie, sir.
9. I'm o'er young, &c.

10. My mammie coft me a new gown,
11. The kirk maun hae the gracing o't;
12. Were I to lie wi' you, kind Sir,
13. I'm feared ye'd spoil the lacing o't.
14. I'm o'er young, &c.

15. Hallowmass is come and gane,
16. The nights are lang in winter, sir,
17. And you an' I in ae bed,
18. In trowth, I dare na venture, sir.
19. I'm o'er young, &c.

20. Fu' loud an' shill the frosty wind
21. Blaws thro' the leafless timmer, sir;
22. But if ye come this gate again;
23. I'll aulder be gin simmer, sir.
24. I'm o'er young, &c.
