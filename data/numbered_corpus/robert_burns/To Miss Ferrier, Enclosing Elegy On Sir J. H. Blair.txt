1. NAE heathen name shall I prefix,
2. Frae Pindus or Parnassus;
3. Auld Reekie dings them a' to sticks,
4. For rhyme-inspiring lasses.


5. Jove's tunefu' dochters three times three
6. Made Homer deep their debtor;
7. But, gien the body half an e'e,
8. Nine Ferriers wad done better!


9. Last day my mind was in a bog,
10. Down George's Street I stoited;
11. A creeping cauld prosaic fog
12. My very sense doited.


13. Do what I dought to set her free,
14. My saul lay in the mire;
15. Ye turned a neuk—I saw your e'e—
16. She took the wing like fire!


17. The mournfu' sang I here enclose,
18. In gratitude I send you,
19. And pray, in rhyme as weel as prose,
20. A' gude things may attend you!
