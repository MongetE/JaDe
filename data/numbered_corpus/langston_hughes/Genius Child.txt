1. This is a song for the genius child.
2. Sing it softly, for the song is wild.
3. Sing it softly as ever you can -
4. Lest the song get out of hand.

5. Nobody loves a genius child.

6. Can you love an eagle,
7. Tame or wild?
8. Can you love an eagle,
9. Wild or tame?
10. Can you love a monster
11. Of frightening name?

12. Nobody loves a genius child.

13. Kill him - and let his soul run wild.
