1. I know I am
2. The Negro Problem
3. Being wined and dined,
4. Answering the usual questions
5. That come to white mind
6. Which seeks demurely
7. To Probe in polite way
8. The why and wherewithal
9. Of darkness U.S.A.--
10. Wondering how things got this way
11. In current democratic night,
12. Murmuring gently
13. Over fraises du bois,
14. "I'm so ashamed of being white."

15. The lobster is delicious,
16. The wine divine,
17. And center of attention
18. At the damask table, mine.
19. To be a Problem on
20. Park Avenue at eight
21. Is not so bad.
22. Solutions to the Problem,
23. Of course, wait.
