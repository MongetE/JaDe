1. My name is Johnson--
2. Madam Alberta K.
3. The Madam stands for business.
4. I'm smart that way.

5. I had a
6. HAIR-DRESSING PARLOR
7. Before
8. The depression put
9. The prices lower.

10. Then I had a
11. BARBECUE STAND
12. Till I got mixed up
13. With a no-good man.

14. Cause I had a insurance
15. The WPA
16. Said, We can't use you
17. Wealthy that way.

18. I said,
19. DON'T WORRY 'BOUT ME!
20. Just like the song,
21. You WPA folks take care of yourself--
22. And I'll get along.

23. I do cooking,
24. Day's work, too!
25. Alberta K. Johnson--
26. Madam to you.
