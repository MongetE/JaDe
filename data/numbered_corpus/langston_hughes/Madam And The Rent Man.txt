1. The rent man knocked.
2. He said, Howdy-do?
3. I said, What
4. Can I do for you?
5. He said, You know
6. Your rent is due.

7. I said, Listen,
8. Before I'd pay
9. I'd go to Hades
10. And rot away!

11. The sink is broke,
12. The water don't run,
13. And you ain't done a thing
14. You promised to've done.

15. Back window's cracked,
16. Kitchen floor squeaks,
17. There's rats in the cellar,
18. And the attic leaks.

19. He said, Madam,
20. It's not up to me.
21. I'm just the agent,
22. Don't you see?

23. I said, Naturally,
24. You pass the buck.
25. If it's money you want
26. You're out of luck.

27. He said, Madam,
28. I ain't pleased!
29. I said, Neither am I.
30. So we agrees!
