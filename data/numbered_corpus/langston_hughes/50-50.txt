1. I’m all alone in this world, she said,
2. Ain’t got nobody to share my bed,
3. Ain’t got nobody to hold my hand—
4. The truth of the matter’s
5. I ain’t got no man.

6. Big Boy opened his mouth and said,
7. Trouble with you is
8. You ain’t got no head!
9. If you had a head and used your mind
10. You could have me with you
11. All the time.

12. She answered, Babe, what must I do?

13. He said, Share your bed—
14. And your money, too.
