1. Done into Verse,  1653.


2. Bless'd is the man who hath not walk'd astray
3. In counsel of the wicked, and ith'way
4. Of sinners hath not stood, and in the seat
5. Of scorners hath not sate.  But in the great
6. Jehovahs Law is ever his delight,
7. And in his law he studies day and night.
8. He shall be as a tree which planted grows
9. By watry streams, and in his season knows
10. To yield his fruit, and his leaf shall not fall.
11. And what he takes in hand shall prosper all.
12. Not so the wicked, but as chaff which fann'd
13. The wind drives, so the wicked shall not stand
14. In judgment, or abide their tryal then
15. Nor sinners in th'assembly of just men.
16. For the Lord knows th'upright way of the just
17. And the way of bad men to ruine must.
