1. VIII

2. Captain or Colonel, or Knight in Arms,
3. Whose chance on these defenceless dores may sease,
4. If ever deed of honour did thee please,
5. Guard them, and him within protect from harms,
6. He can requite thee, for he knows the charms
7. That call Fame on such gentle acts as these,
8. And he can spred thy Name o're Lands and Seas,
9. What ever clime the Suns bright circle warms.
10. Lift not thy spear against the Muses Bowre,
11. The great Emathian Conqueror bid spare
12. The house of Pindarus, when Temple and Towre
13. Went to the ground: And the repeated air
14. Of sad Electra's Poet had the power
15. To save th' Athenian Walls from ruine bare.

16. Notes:
17. Camb. autograph supplies title, When the assault was intended
18. to the city.
