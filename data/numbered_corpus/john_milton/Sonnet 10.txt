1. X

2. Daughter to that good Earl, once President
3. Of Englands Counsel, and her Treasury,
4. Who liv'd in both, unstain'd with gold or fee,
5. And left them both, more in himself content,
6. Till the sad breaking of that Parlament
7. Broke him, as that dishonest victory
8. At Chaeronea, fatal to liberty
9. Kil'd with report that Old man eloquent,
10. Though later born, then to have known the dayes
11. Wherin your Father flourisht, yet by you
12. Madam, me thinks I see him living yet;
13. So well your words his noble vertues praise,
14. That all both judge you to relate them true,
15. And to possess them, Honour'd Margaret.

16. Note: Camb. autograph supplies title, To the Lady Margaret
17. Ley.
