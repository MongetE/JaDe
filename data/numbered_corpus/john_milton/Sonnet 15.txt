1. XV

2. On The Late Massacher In Piemont

3. Avenge O lord thy slaughter'd Saints, whose bones
4. Lie scatter'd on the Alpine mountains cold,
5. Ev'n them who kept thy truth so pure of old
6. When all our Fathers worship't Stocks and Stones,
7. Forget not: in thy book record their groanes
8. Who were thy Sheep and in their antient Fold
9. Slayn by the bloody Piemontese that roll'd
10. Mother with Infant down the Rocks.  Their moans
11. The Vales redoubl'd to the Hills, and they
12. To Heav'n.  Their martyr'd blood and ashes sow
13. O're all th'Italian fields where still doth sway
14. The triple Tyrant: that from these may grow
15. A hunder'd-fold, who having learnt thy way
16. Early may fly the Babylonian wo.
